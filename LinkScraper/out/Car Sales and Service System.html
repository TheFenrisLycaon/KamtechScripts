Other features of Car Sales And Service System, while adding items from admin account the user has to provide Car name, Car price and Car quantity. Similarly while refilling Cars, the user has to enter the Car name to select the Car, then he/she has to enter quantity. Removing an Car is also too easy, he/she just has to open the report and hit the delete Car. The system displays Particulars (Car name) with Available stock and Price while viewing stocks.

We have developed a Cars sales dashboard, where admin can enter the customer name and contact numnber to start Car sales. The main feature is dynamic Car sales calculation, where admin need to choose Car from a dropdown and that Car will be added into the cart with cost automatically.
