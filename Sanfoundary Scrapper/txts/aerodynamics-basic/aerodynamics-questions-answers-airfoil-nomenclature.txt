1. Who designed the nomenclature of airfoil?
a) NACA series
b) EULAR series
c) EPPLER series
d) CLARK series
a
2. Purpose of camber in an airfoil is ____________
a) to increase maximum drag
b) to increase maximum lift
c) to decrease maximum lift
d) to decrease maximum drag
b
3. Is shape of the airfoil depends on thickness?
a) True
b) False
a
4. What is a chord?
a) Distance between leading edge and chord
b) Distance between chord and chamber
c) Distance between leading edge and trailing edge
d) Distance between trailing edge and chord
c
5. Purpose of leading edge is to ______________
a) allow the wing to operate at high angle of attack
b) allow the wing to operate at low angle of attack
c) allow the wing to operate at stall condition
d) allow the wing to operate in level condition
a
6. What is the purpose of trailing edge?
a) Airflow rejoins
b) Airflow separated
c) Vortex are created
d) Stalling will created
a
7. What is the shape of the airfoil at the leading edge?
a) Semi-circular
b) Curve
c) Straight
d) Circular
d
8. What is the thickness in NACA 2412 airfoil?
a) 12%
b) 24%
c) 41%
d) 2%
a
9. What parameter is used to decrease the stall speed?
a) Chord
b) Chord line
c) Camber
d) Camber line
c
10. What is the thickness in NACA 747A315 airfoil?
a) 15%
b) 25%
c) 30%
d) 7%
a
11. Why NACA 0012 airfoil is said to be symmetric airfoil?
a) Because of no camber
b) Because of camber
c) Because of no thickness
d) Because of chord line
a
