1. The flows in which all the flow parameters are the function of ‘x’ is called as _________
a) 3D flow
b) 2D flow
c) Quasi 1D flow
d) Quasi 2D flow
c
2. Incompressible flow is the one in which density is ___________
a) 0
b) constant
c) varies from point to point
d) negligible
b
3. In a wind tunnel, the section with minimum area is called as __________
a) exit
b) nozzle
c) inlet
d) throat
d
4. The below figure shows which of the following wind tunnel?
a) Convergent duct
b) Divergent duct
c) Convergent-divergent duct
d) Simple duct
c
5. In a venturi duct, the pressure at throat is __________
a) lower than ambient pressure
b) equal to ambient pressure
c) greater than ambient pressure
d) 0
a
6. The carburetor of an automobile engine makes use of _____
a) venturi duct
b) convergent duct
c) divergent duct
d) wind tunnel
a
7. Which of the following parameter can be measured using venturi?
a) temperature
b) velocity
c) volume
d) density
a
8. Which of the following is an application of incompressible flow in a duct?
a) High speed wind tunnel
b) Low speed wind tunnel
c) Flow in air
d) Cannot be said
b
9. The open circuit wind tunnel is similar to venturi duct ______
a) True
b) False
a
10. Inside a wind tunnel, the area further decreases from A2 to A3, that section is called as _______
a) Converging section
b) Divergent section
c) Throat section
d) Nozzle
b
