1. Why is analyzing hypersonic bodies dependent on the use of computational fluid dynamics (CFD)?
a) Lack of experimental facility
b) Complex to analyse hypersonic body
c) Time consuming
d) Hypersonic flight regimes are hypothetical
a
2. Experimental work on hypersonic bodies involves testing Mach number, Reynolds number and temperature simultaneously.
a) True
b) False
b
3. Which of these characteristics vary as a result of chemically reacting gas compared to the perfect gas?
a) Pitching moment coefficient
b) Temperature
c) Drag
d) Lift
a
4. Which of these is not the reason to resort to CFD for solving hypersonic problems?
a) Inexpensive
b) Fast
c) Accurate
d) Complex use
d
5. Which of these approaches is not used to find the solution of the fully viscous flowfield in hypersonic flow?
a) Viscous shock-layer solutions
b) Parabolized Navier-Stokes solution
c) Full Navier-Stokes equation
d) Boundary-layer solution
d
