1. Inconel is an alloy that contains nickel and ___________
a) iron
b) steel
c) zinc
d) chromium
d
2. The atomic number of nickel is __________
a) 5
b) 40
c) 28
d) 13
c
3. Which one of the following is not a nickel ore?
a) Pentlandite
b) Chalcopyrite
c) Rutile
d) Pyrrhotite
c
4. Nickel alloys have very bad corrosion resistance.
a) True
b) False
b
5. Monel is an alloy that consists of nickel and __________
a) brass
b) copper
c) bronze
d) titanium
b
6. The young’s modulus of nickel is __________
a) 69 GPa
b) 120 GPa
c) 200 GPa
d) 159 GPa
c
7. K Monel is a non-magnetic material.
a) True
b) False
a
8. Nickel is a __________ material.
a) soft
b) non-ductile
c) non-metallic
d) ductile
d
9. ___________ is often used in the manufacturing of aircraft engine exhaust collectors.
a) K Monel
b) Monel
c) Inconel
d) T Monel
c
10. K Monel is an alloy that includes nickel __________
a) copper
b) aluminium
c) iron
d) copper-aluminium
d
11. The melting point of nickel is __________
a) 660°C
b) 1445°C
c) 1668°C
d) 1300°C
b
