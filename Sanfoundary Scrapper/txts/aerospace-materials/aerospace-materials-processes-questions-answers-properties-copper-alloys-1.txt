1. Muntz metal consists of ___________ copper and __________ zinc.
a) 75%, 25%
b) 25%, 75%
c) 50%, 50%
d) 60%, 40%
d
2. Manganese bronze is remarkably ___________
a) difficult to forge
b) weak
c) strong
d) low in zinc
c
3. Brass that consists of around ___________ zinc is highly ductile in nature.
a) 49-50%
b) 15-18%
c) 30-35%
d) 1-2%
c
4. The ultimate tensile strength for heat-treated muntz metal is __________
a) 80,000 p.s.i
b) 20,000 p.s.i
c) 60,000 p.s.i
d) 57,000 p.s.i
a
5. Brass is primarily made of copper and aluminium.
a) True
b) False
b
6. Manganese bronze is made of around 57% to 60% of __________
a) lead
b) manganese
c) copper
d) zinc
c
7. The density of beryllium copper is __________
a) 8.24 gram per cubic centimeters
b) 26 gram per cubic centimeters
c) 13.24 gram per cubic centimeters
d) 4.98 gram per cubic centimeters
a
8. Which of the following are properties of beryllium copper?
a) Low-strength, non-magnetic
b) Low-strength, magnetic
c) Magnetic, high-strength
d) High-strength, non-magnetic
d
9. A casting type of manganese bronze called manganese-aluminium alloy includes approximately __________ of tin.
a) 50%
b) 0.2%
c) 0.5%
d) 5%
c
10. Beryllium copper consists of around ___________ of beryllium.
a) 0.5%
b) 2 to 2.25%
c) 10 to 15%
d) 26 to30%
b
11. The ultimate tensile strength of sand cast Hy-Ten-Sl-Bronze is __________
a) 75,000 p.s.i
b) 115,000 p.s.i
c) 999,000 p.s.i
d) 229,000 p.s.i
b
12. Manganese bronze possesses the property of being corrosion resistant.
a) True
b) False
a
13. Which of the following are properties of Hy-Ten-Sl-Bronze?
a) wear-prone, corrosive
b) wear-resistant, non-corrosive
c) wear-resistant, corrosive
d) wear-prone, non-corrosive
b
14. A type of casting of manganese bronze called manganese-aluminium alloy consists of approximately __________ of aluminium
a) 60 to 68%
b) 2 to 4%
c) 3 to 7.5%
d) 15%
c
15. Which of the following elements is occasionally present in brass is minor amounts?
a) Cooper, lead
b) Iron, vanadium
c) Lead
d) Iron, lead
d
