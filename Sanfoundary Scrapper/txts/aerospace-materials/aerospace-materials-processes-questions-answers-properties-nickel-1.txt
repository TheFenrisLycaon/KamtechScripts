1. The approximate percentage of chromium present in Inconel is __________
a) 42%
b) 6.5%
c) 0.25%
d) 13%
d
2. Machining of the nickel alloy Monel can be done __________
a) with hardships
b) trapped
c) without hardship
d) impossibly
c
3. The melting point of K Monel is around ___________
a) 2400-2460°C
b) 1245°F
c) 2400-2460°F
d) 1064-1890°F
c
4. Monel is used with great frequency in manufacturing aircrafts.
a) True
b) False
b
5. The density of Inconel is ___________
a) 22 gram per cubic centimeter
b) 8.51 gram per cubic centimeter
c) 123 gram per cubic centimeter
d) 45 gram per cubic centimeter
b
6. The approximate amount of aluminium present in K Monel is ___________
a) 26%
b) 0.15%
c) 2.75%
d) 99%
c
7. Inconel is considered to be a non-ferrous material.
a) True
b) False
a
8. Monel is utilized in ___________
a) non-chemical applications
b) schools
c) aerospace purposes
d) industries
d
9. Inconel is a heat _____________ material.
a) conducting
b) accepting
c) resisting
d) exhausting
c
10. It is possible to harden K Monel through ___________
a) clod treatment
b) gold treatment
c) proof treatment
d) heat treatment
d
11. The density of rolled Monel is __________
a) 19.1 gram per cubic centimeter
b) 8.9 gram per cubic centimeter
c) 32 gram per cubic centimeter
d) 8.8 gram per cubic centimeter
b
12. K Monel is typically manufactured by adding a little __________ in Monel.
a) aluminium
b) titanium
c) copper
d) silicon
a
13. K Monel is a ___________ material.
a) ferrous
b) non-ferrous
c) proxy-ferrous
d) anti-ferrous
b
14. Monel consists of approximately _________ of copper.
a) 30%
b) 1.4%
c) 0.1%
d) 16%
a
15. Welding on Inconel is done __________
a) with difficulty
b) easily
c) hardly
d) wretchedly
b
