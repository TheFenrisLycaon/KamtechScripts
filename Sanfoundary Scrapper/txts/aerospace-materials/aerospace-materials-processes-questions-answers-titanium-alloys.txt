1. Titanium is __________ in color.
a) red
b) white
c) reddish-brown
d) silvery-white
d
2. Titanium has __________ density.
a) low
b) high
c) moderate
d) zero
a
3. The atomic number for titanium is _________
a) 26
b) 196
c) 22
d) 13
c
4. The melting point of titanium is _________
a) 3272°F
b) 1220°F
c) 2795°F
d) 3272°C
a
5. Titanium consists of about _________ of the earth’s crust.
a) 27.7%
b) 0.57%
c) 2.34%
d) 5%
b
6. In which of the following countries are titanium abundantly found in?
a) Ukraine
b) Greenland
c) Antarctica
d) Canada, Ukraine
d
7. Titanium will not be found in a __________ state.
a) uncombined
b) sunny
c) combined
d) typical
a
8. The primary ores of titanium are _________
a) rutile, sulphur
b) ilmenite, bauxite
c) bauxite, rutile
d) rutile, ilmenite
d
9. The atomic weight of titanium is ___________
a) 55.84g
b) 26.97g
c) 47.9g
d) 35.5g
c
10. Titanium exists in one crystal form.
a) True
b) False
b
11. Upon heat treatment, titanium can experience loss of ductility.
a) True
b) False
a
