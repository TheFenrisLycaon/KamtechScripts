1. Following diagram represents ____________
a) conformal
b) internal weapon carriage
c) external weapon carriage
d) fully submerged
a
2. Following diagram represents __________
a) internal weapon carriage
b) non submerged weapon carriage
c) external weapon carriage
d) conformal
a
3. Following diagram represents __________
a) typical gun
b) missile layout
c) rocket system
d) missile launch system
a
4. Which weapon carriage option gives more flexibility to carry different weapons?
a) Internal carriage
b) External carriage
c) Conformal
d) Rail launch system always
b
5. If weapon is mounted at bottom of the fuselage then it is called __________
a) internal carriage
b) external always
c) conformal
d) rail launch system always
c
6. Which of the following is a disadvantage of semi-submerged weapon carriage?
a) Indentation will increase structural weight in this type of weapon carriage
b) Lowest possible weight
c) Drag will be more than external carriage
d) Drag will be lower than that of the external carriage
a
7. Which of the following will produce the lowest drag?
a) Internal carriage
b) External always
c) Conformal
d) Rail launch system always
a
8. Following diagram represents ____________
a) rotary weapons bay
b) linear motion
c) cabin
d) cockpit
a
9. Gun should be located near to the centerline of aircraft.
a) True
b) False
a
10. Determine the corrections or otherwise of the following Assertion [A] and Reason [R]:
Assertion [A]: Typically, gun should be located away from the cockpit.
Reason [R]: Gun can induce sudden yawing.
a) Both [A] and [R] are true and [R] is the correct reason for [A] 
b) Both [A] and [R] are true but [R] is not the correct reason for [A] 
c) [A] is true but [R] is false
d) [A] is false but [R] is true
b
11. Which of the following is an advantage of internal weapon carriage?
a) Weapon RCS will tend to be none
b) Highest possible RCS
c) RCS is more than external
d) RCS is more than conformal
a
