1. Passenger cabin is defined by ________
a) pitch, headroom, aisle, etc
b) pitch only
c) headroom only
d) aisle only
a
2. Pitch is defined as ___________
a) Distance from the back of one seat to the back of next respective seat
b) Height of seat
c) Height of seat minus height of next respective seat
d) Length of one seat plus height of seat
a
3. What is the headroom?
a) Vertical distance from the floor to the roof cover seat
b) Height of seat
c) Height of seat measured height of next respective seat
d) Length of one seat plus height of seat
a
4. What do you mean by aisle?
a) Passage between two rows
b) Height of seat from floor
c) Height of seat measured height of next respective seat
d) Length of one seat plus height of seat of second row
a
5. An aircraft has 6 seats in one row then, find the ideal value of aisle numbers.
a) 2
b) 5
c) 3
d) 9
a
Typical number of aisle required = 6/3 – 1= 2-1 = 1.

6. A small business jet has internal diameter of 32in. find the external diameter of passenger compartment.
a) 34 in
b) 35 in
c) 32m
d) 34m
a
For a small business jet typical value of t is 1in.
Hence, D=d+2t = 32+2*1= 34in.

10. A jumbo jet has external diameter of 150in. Calculate the value of internal diameter for passenger cabin.
a) 142in
b) 167in
c) 172in
d) 150in
a
Door jumbo jet, structural thickness t = 4in.
Hence, internal diameter d is given by, d = D – 2*t = 150 – 2*4 = 142in.
