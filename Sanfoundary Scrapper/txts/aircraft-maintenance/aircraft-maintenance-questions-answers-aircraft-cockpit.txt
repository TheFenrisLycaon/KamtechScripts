1. In an aircraft, the cockpit is usually referred to as the _________
a) Aircraft deck
b) Flight deck
c) Control deck
d) Operating deck
b
2. When was the word cockpit first used in context to airplanes?
a) 1994
b) 2004
c) 1914
d) 1814
c
3. What is the full form for HOTAS?
a) Hands on Throttle and Stick
b) Hands on Throttle and Stand
c) Hands on Thrust and Stand
d) Hands on Throttle and Slats
a
4. Which of the following is not a function provided by HOTAS?
a) Radio communications
b) Nose wheel steering
c) Speed brake controls
d) Landing gear controls
d
5. Which of the following was the first airplane with a completely enclosed cabin for pilots?
a) Avro Type F
b) MD-80
c) Blériot VIII
d) Bell X-1
a
6. The right seat in an aircraft cockpit is the seat used by the co-pilot.
a) True
b) False
a
7. What is the full form of FCU?
a) Flight control usage
b) Flight center unit
c) Flight control unit
d) Flight center usage
c
8. Which of the following shows some form of heading indicator?
a) PFD
b) FCU
c) ND
d) ECAM
a
9. What is the full form of DVI?
a) Digital voice input
b) Direct voice input
c) Discrete voice input
d) Damp voice input
b
10. Controls are incorporated onto the stick and throttle to enable the pilot to maintain a head-up position.
a) True
b) False
a
11. Which of the following is not a cockpit design discipline?
a) Neuroscience
b) Bioscience
c) Human Factors Engineering
d) Cognitive science
b
12. The MFD is usually referred to as the ________
a) Glare shield panel
b) Glare panel
c) Shield panel
d) Display panel
a
13. Which of the following is a standard that defines the interface between an independent cockpit display system and the avionics equipment?
a) ARINC
b) ARINC 661
c) EICAS
d) ECAM
b
14. Which of the following is not shown by backup instruments?
a) Speed
b) Altitude
c) Heading
d) Passengers onboard
d
15. A ND may be pilot selectable to swap with the PFD.
a) True
b) False
a
