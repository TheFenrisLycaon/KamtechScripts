1. ESD in common terminology refers to ___________
a) Electrostatic discharge
b) Electric discharge
c) Electronic discharge
d) Elastic discharge
a
2. Electrostatic sensitive device is a component which can be damaged by common _________
a) Dynamic charges
b) Static charges
c) Electric charges
d) Electronic charges
b
3. What is the electrical resistance of conductive ESD protective materials?
a) Between 1mΩ and 1MΩ
b) Between 1kΩ and 1MΩ
c) Between 10kΩ and 10MΩ
d) Between 10mΩ and 10MΩ
b
4. Which of the following is a material that attenuates current and electrical fields?
a) Anti-static
b) Conductive
c) Shielding
d) Dissipative
c
5. Dissipative materials have an electrical resistance between 1kΩ and 1TΩ.
a) False
b) True
a

a) Low charging
b) High charging
c) Null charging
d) Dash charging

7. Which of the following is an alternate to photoemission?
a) Shielding
b) Proton emission
c) Corona discharge
d) Electrical grounding

8. Which of the following is a device that reduces or inhibits electrostatic discharge?
a) Dynamic device
b) Static device
c) Anti-static device
d) Anti-dynamic device
c
9. Which of the following is used for the neutralization of static charges in a larger area?
a) Photoemission
b) Antistatic agents
c) Electrical grounding
d) Static agents

10. Human beings have natural electrical sources running through the body.
a) False
b) True
b
11. An ionizing bar is sometimes referred to as a _________
a) Anti dynamic bar
b) Antistatic bar
c) Dynamic bar
d) Static bar

a) Yellow
b) Pink
