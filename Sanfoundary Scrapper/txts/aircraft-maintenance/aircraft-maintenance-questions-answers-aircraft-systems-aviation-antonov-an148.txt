1. What is the seating layout in a one class An-148-100 aircraft?
a) 2-3
b) 3-3
c) 1-1
d) 2-2
a
2. The An-148 uses ________ engines.
a) Turboprop
b) Turbojet
c) Turbofan
d) Turboprop
c
3. Which of the following is a stretched version of the An-148?
a) Airbus A320
b) Antonov An-225
c) Antonov An-158
d) Airbus A319
c
4. The An-148 can carry up to _________ passengers.
a) 120
b) 85
c) 360
d) 25
b
5. The An-148 first flew on ________
a) 17 December 1995
b) 17 December 2000
c) 17 December 2014
d) 17 December 2004
d

b) False

b) 10.7 tons
c) 90.7 tons

a) An-148-100E
b) An-158
c) An-178

c
9. The An-148 has a service ceiling of __________

b) False
