1. What is the full form of WSR?
a) Wright surveillance radar
b) Wing surveillance radar
c) Weight surveillance radar
d) Weather surveillance radar
d
2. Which of the following waves are used by radar?
a) Ultrasonic
b) Radio
c) Nuclear
d) Electronic
b
3. Modern weather radars being used in aircrafts are usually __________ Doppler radars.
a) Heat
b) Pressure
c) Pulse
d) Sound
c
4. Weather radars used in aircrafts emit directional pulses of ___________ radiation.
a) Ultrasonic
b) Microwave
c) Supersonic
d) Nuclear
b
5. Radar pulses are the order of a nanosecond long.
a) False
b) True
a
6. In Canada, the 5 cm weather radar systems use angles ranging from 0.3 to _________ degrees.
a) 2.5
b) 25
c) 2.25
d) 250
b
7. Which of the following radar system is used only for short-range units?
a) X-band
b) C-band
c) Ka-band
d) S-band
a
8. The duration of the listen cycle is on the order of a _________
a) Second
b) Nanosecond
c) Millisecond
d) Microsecond
c
9. The cavity magnetron was developed in _________
a) Australia
b) Germany
c) United Kingdom
d) The United States
c
10. The radar returns are generally described by color or level on an aircraft radar display.
a) False
b) True
b
11. On a radar display, magenta indicates _________
a) Light precipitation
b) Moderate precipitation
c) Heavy precipitation
d) Extremely heavy precipitation
d
