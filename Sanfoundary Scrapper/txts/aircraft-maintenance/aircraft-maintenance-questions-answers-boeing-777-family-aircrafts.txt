1. Which of the following is the world’s largest twinjet?
a) Boeing 777
b) Boeing 747
c) Boeing 737
d) Boeing 787
a
2. When is the 777X scheduled to enter service?
a) 2025
b) 2030
c) 2020
d) 2022
c
3. Which of the following is not an engine choice for the 777?
a) Rolls-Royce Trent 800
b) General Electric GE90
c) Pratt & Whitney PW4000
d) Rolls-Royce Trent 700
d
4. Which of the following was the launch customer of the 777?
a) EasyJet
b) United Airlines
c) Qatar Airways
d) Virgin Atlantic
b
5. Which of the following is the only customer to have operated all 777 variants produced?
a) Thai Airways
b) Air India
c) British Airways
d) Emirates
d
6. The 777 is Boeing’s first fly-by-wire airliner.
a) True
b) False
a
7. The wings of the 777 also serve as fuel storage.
a) True
b) False
a
8. What is the typical economy class layout in a Boeing 777?
a) 3-2-3
b) 3-3-3
c) 3-4-3
d) 2-4-2
c
9. The wings of a 777 are swept back at __________ degrees for a cruise speed of Mach 0.83.
a) 316 degrees
b) 31.6 degrees
c) 0.316 degrees
d) 3.16 degrees
b
10. The Boeing 777 offers a range up to 10,000nmi.
a) True
b) False
b
11. Which of the following aircraft holds the record for the longest non-stop flight of a passenger airliner?
a) Boeing 777-200LR
b) Boeing 777-200ER
c) Boeing 777F
d) Boeing 777X
a
12. What is the maximum speed of the 777?
a) Mach 0.87
b) Mach 0.087
c) Mach 8.7
d) Mach 87
a
13. Boeing delivered the 100th 777 to __________
a) Air Canada
b) Malaysia Airlines
c) Vistara
d) Emirates
d
14. The 777F first flew on __________
a) July 14, 2019
b) July 14, 2000
c) July 14, 2008
d) July 14, 2010
c
15. What is the ICAO designation for the Boeing 777-300?
a) B773
b) B772
c) B777
d) B300
a
