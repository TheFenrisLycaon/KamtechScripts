1. The Boeing CH-47 Chinook is a __________ engined rotorcraft.
a) Mono
b) Quad
c) Tri
d) Twin
d
2. When did the Boeing CH-47 Chinook first fly?
a) 21 September 1990
b) 21 September 1985
c) 21 September 1961
d) 21 September 2000
c
3. The preproduction CH-47 was designated as __________
a) YCH-1B
b) HC-1B
c) AC-1B
d) CCH-1B
a
4. The CH-47D entered service in __________
a) 1965
b) 1982
c) 2005
d) 1996
b
5. Which of the following is the civilian version of the Chinook?
a) HH-47
b) Boeing Vertol 234
c) CH-47A
d) CH-47CC
b
6. The CH-47 is powered by turboshaft engines.
a) True
b) False
a
7. The Boeing CH-47 was named Chinook after the Chinook people of the Pacific Northwest.
a) True
b) False
a
8. The CH-47 Chinook has been licensed to be built by __________ in Japan.
a) Agusta
b) Phillips
c) Kawasaki
d) Fujifilm
c
9. The CH-47F is powered by two __________ horsepower engines.
a) 6,733
b) 4,733
c) 2,365
d) 50%
b
10. The earlier Chinook design was limited by its rotor system.
a) True
b) False
a
11. The Boeing CH-47A had a maximum gross weight of __________
a) 40,000lb
b) 25,000lb
c) 35,000lb
d) 33,000lb
d
12. How many CH-47A were built?
a) 349
b) 39
c) 400
d) 68
a

a) CHH-47B
b) ACH-47A
c) CH-47C
d) MH-47D
b

a) 575
b) 1,750
c) 8,750
