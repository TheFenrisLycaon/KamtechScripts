1. The Boeing 747 Dreamlifter aircraft is also known as the Boeing 747-400 __________
a) Large Cargo Glider
b) Low Cargo Freighter
c) Large Cargo Freighter
d) Low Cargo Glider
c
2. When was the maiden flight of the 747 Dreamlifter?
a) 9 September 2006
b) 9 September 2016
c) 9 September 1990
d) 9 September 1984
a
3. The initial Boeing 747 Dreamlifter program intended to produce only _________ aircrafts.
a) 3
b) 5
c) 13
d) 8
a
4. The 747 Dreamlifter has a cruise speed of __________
a) Mach 0.90
b) Mach 0.82
c) Mach 0.72
d) Mach 0.85
b
5. Which of the following is not an airline from which Boeing acquired the four second hand 747-400s?
a) Emirates
b) China Airlines
c) Air China
d) Malaysia Airlines
a
6. The 747 Dreamlifter is exclusively used for transporting Boeing 787 aircraft components.
a) True
b) False
a
7. The 787 aircraft parts were deemed too large for standard marine shipping.
a) True
b) False
a
8. When was the first 747 LCF rolled out of the hangar?
a) 17 August 2000
b) 17 August 2006
c) 17 August 2016
