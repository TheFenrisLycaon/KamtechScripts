1. The clear-channel stations are required to broadcast at higher powers of ___________
a) 10 mW to 50 mW
b) 10 MW to 50 MW
c) 10 W to 50 W
d) 10 kW to 50 kW
d
2. Radio waves at Medium Frequency wavelengths propagate via ___________
a) Ground waves
b) Air waves
c) Sky waves
d) Electric waves
