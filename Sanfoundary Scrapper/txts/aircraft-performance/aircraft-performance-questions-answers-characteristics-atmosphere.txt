1. What is the ideal gas equation?
a) P=nRT
b) PV=nRT
c) PV=ρRT
d) Pρ=RT
b
P=pressure of gas (Units=N/m2)
V=volume of gas (Units=m3)
ρ=density of gas (Units=Kg/m3)
R=universal gas constant (Units=Nm/kg-K)
T=temperature of gas (Units=K).
