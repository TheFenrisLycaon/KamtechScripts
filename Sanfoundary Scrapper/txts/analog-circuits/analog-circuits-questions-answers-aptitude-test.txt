6.  If Vth is .45V, what is the output voltage for the following C.S. stage?
a) 4.7 V
b) 3.9 V
c) 2.1 V
d) 3.5 V
a
i. Vout = gm*R4 = 2*Id/(Vgs – Vth)*R4
ii. Vout = Vdd – Id * R4
We have the values of all the parameters. Solving for Vout will yield Vout as 4.7V.
