1. The region greater than \(\frac{2D^2}{λ}\) in an antenna with largest dimension D is called _____
a) Far field region
b) Near filed region
c) Radiative Field
d) Reactive Field
a
2. Which of the following is known as Non Radiative region?
a) Far field region
b) Near filed region
c) Radiative Field
d) Reactive Field
d
3. Which of the following is true?
a) Radiative Field < 0.62\(\sqrt{\frac{D^3}{λ}}\) 
b) Reactive Field < 0.62\(\sqrt{\frac{D^3}{λ}}\) 
c) Fresnel Field << 0.62\(\sqrt{\frac{D^2}{λ}}\) 
d) Near field > \(\frac{2D^2}{λ}\) 
b
4. Radiative and Non Radiative fields are parts of which field region?
a) Near field
b) Fresnel field
c) Reactive field
d) Far field
a
5. Near field is also known as inductive field.
a) True
b) False
a
