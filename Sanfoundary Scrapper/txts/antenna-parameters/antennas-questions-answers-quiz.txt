1. Beam area and directivity are related as _____
a) \(D=\frac{2\pi}{B}\) 
b) \(D=\frac{4\pi}{B}\) 
c) \(D=\frac{B}{2\pi}\)

\(D=\frac{4\pi}{B}\)
