1. Graphite has layered _________ structure.
a) Three dimensional structure
b) Pyramidal
c) Planar
d) Bipyramidal
c
2. The distance between the planes of the graphite is _________
a) 0.876nm
b) 0.223nm
c) 0.124nm
d) 0.335nm
d
3. Bonding between the layers is _________
a) Strong
b) Weak
c) Very tightly bonded
d) Not bonded
b
4. The graphite is _________ anisotropic.
a) Highly
b) Lightly
c) Not
d) Very less
a
5. In oxygen containing atmospheres, the graphite readily converted into _________
a) Carbon-monoxide
b) Carbondioxide
c) Carbonmonosulphide
d) Carbon-disulphide
b
6. Graphite is used in the arc lamp electrode.
a) True
b) False
a
7. The graphite undergoes ________ corrosion in stainless steel.
a) Microbial corrosion
b) Crevice corrosion
c) Pitting corrosion
d) High temperature corrosion
c
8. The graphite is __________
a) Paramagnetic
b) Diamagnetic
c) Ferromagnetic
d) Ferrimagnetic
b
9. Graphite is also called as ________
a) White lead
b) Black lead
c) White coal
d) Black coal
b
10. Natural graphite is mostly used in _________
a) Refrigerators
b) Laboratories
c) Catalyst
d) Fuel
a
