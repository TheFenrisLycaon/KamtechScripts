1. 3-phosphoglycerate is not the metabolic precursor for ____________
a) Serine
b) Glycine
c) Cysteine
d) Arginine
d
2. Pyruvate is the precursor for ____________

b) Glutamate
c) Serine

d) Serine

a) Proline
b) Glutamine
c) Serine

c

b) Threonine
