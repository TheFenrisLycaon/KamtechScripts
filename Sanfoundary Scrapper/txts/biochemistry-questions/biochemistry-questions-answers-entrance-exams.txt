1. A chlorophyll-like accessory pigment lacking Mg+2 is ____________
a) Pheophytin
b) Cytochrome c
c) Plastocyanin
d) Ferredoxin
a
2. A soluble Cu-containing electron transfer protein is ____________
a) Pheophytin
b) Cytochrome c
c) Plastocyanin
d) Ferredoxin
c
3. Photosystem II is a ____________
a) Pheophytin-quinone type
b) Ferredoxin type
c) Plastocyanin type
d) Plastocyanin-ferredoxin type
a
4. Which of the following describes the complete route by which electrons flow H2O to NADP+?
a) Z scheme
b) A scheme
c) B scheme
d) X scheme
a
5. Plastoquinol formed in PSII is oxidized by ____________
a) Cytochrome b6f
b) Cytochrome c
c) Cytochrome f
d) Cytochrome h
a
6. A mobile, lipid soluble carrier of two electrons present in chloroplasts is ____________
a) PQB
b) Q
c) Cytochrome c
d) Plastocyanin
a
7. A mobile, lipid soluble carrier of two electrons present in mitochondria is ____________
a) PQB
b) Q
c) Cytochrome c
d) Plastocyanin
b
8. A water-soluble protein that carries one electron present in mitochondria is ____________
a) PQB
b) Q
c) Cytochrome c
d) Plastocyanin
c
9. A water-soluble protein that carries one electron present in the chloroplast is ____________
a) PQB
b) Q
c) Cytochrome c
d) Plastocyanin
d
10. Cyanobacteria does not use which of the following for both oxidative and photophosphorylation?
a) Cytochrome c6
b) Cytochrome b6f
c) Plastoquinone
d) Plastocyanin
d
