a) Uridylyltransferase
b) Adenylyltransferase

d) Dinitrogenase

a) Monomer
b) Dimer

a) Uridylyltransferase
b) Adenylyltransferase
c) Glutamate synthase

b) Glutamine

a) Glutamine amidotransferase
b) Adenylyltransferase
c) Glutamate synthase
d) Glutamine synthase

7. Conversion of nitrogen to ammonia or nitrogenous compounds is termed as ____________
a) Nitrogen fixation
b) Nitrification
c) Denitrification
d) Nitrogen assimilation
a
Ammonia or ammonium is oxidized to nitrite followed by the oxidation of nitrite to nitrate is called nitrification.
Nitrate is reduced and ultimately produces N2 through a series of intermediate gaseous nitrogen oxide products is called denitrification.
