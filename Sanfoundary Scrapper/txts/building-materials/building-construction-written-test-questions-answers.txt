1. The symbol given below represents ________
a) Stairs
b) Almirah
c) Window
d) Shutter
b
2. A plan is conventionally cut at the _______
a) Roof level
b) Floor level
c) Plinth level
d) Window sill level
d
3. Windows and almirahs cannot be represented on a plan if it is cut at the plinth level.
a) True
b) False
a

b) Electric bell
c) Lamp
