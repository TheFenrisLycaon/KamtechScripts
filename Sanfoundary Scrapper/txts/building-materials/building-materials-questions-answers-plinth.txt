1. The part of the building above the ground level and up to the floor level immediately above the ground is known as __________
a) Plinth area
b) Formation level
c) Ground level
d) Plinth
d
2. The built up area measured and the plinth level is known as __________
a) Total area
b) Plinth area
c) Plot area
d) Floor area
b
3. What should be the average height of plinth?
a) 300-500 m
b) 1-2 m
c) 300-450 mm
d) 50-100 mm
c
4. In a market area, the plinth area should not exceed __________ of the area of site.
a) 75%
b) 25%
c) 95%
d) 55%
a
5. The ____________ provides support to the occupants, furniture, fixtures and equipments of a building.
a) Plinth
b) Ramp
c) Floor
d) Lifts
c

a) 45 cm
b) 20 cm

a) Plinth area
b) Superstructure
c) Sub-structure
d) Formation level

9. Foundations are more susceptible to ____________
a) Settlement
b) Corrosion
c) Fracture
d) Shocks
