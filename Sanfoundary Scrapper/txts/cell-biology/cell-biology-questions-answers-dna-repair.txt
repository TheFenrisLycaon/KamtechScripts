1. Mutations in the DNA may be passed to the next generation.
a) True
b) False
a
2. How many pathways of nucleotide excision repair exist?
a) 1
b) 2
c) 3
d) 4
b
3. Which repair system operates to remove the altered bases generated by reactive chemicals present in the diet?
a) Base excision repair
b) Nucleotide excision repair
c) Mismatch repair
d) Double-stranded breakage repair
a
4. Which repair system operates to repair DNA strands that are actively transcribed?
a) Double-strand breakage repair
b) Nucleotide excision repair
c) RNA repair
d) Messenger RNA repair
b
5. A mismatched base pair can cause ________________ in the double helix.
a) change in geometry
b) change in location
c) change in size
d) change in alkalinity
a
6. Which of the following does not introduce double-strand breaks in the DNA?
a) Gamma rays
b) Cancer drugs
c) Free radicals
d) Inherited mutations
d
7. The NHEJ pathway is used to rectify __________________ in the DNA.
a) nucleotide substitutions
b) double-strand breaks
c) single-strand breaks
d) mismatched basepairs
b
8. Xeroderma pigmentosum is caused by ______________________
a) UV radiations
b) X-rays
c) Gamma radiations
d) Beta-rays
a
9. How many types of skin cancers can develop by overexposure to ultraviolet rays?
a) 1
b) 2
c) 3
d) 4
