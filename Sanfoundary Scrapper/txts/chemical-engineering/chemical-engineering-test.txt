1. A container has two gases at 27oC, one with molar volume 6 L/mole, another with molar volume 3 L/mole, what is the total pressure of the container?
a)	6.9 atm
b)	8.5 atm
c)	12.3 atm
d)	15.8 atm
View answerAnswer: c
