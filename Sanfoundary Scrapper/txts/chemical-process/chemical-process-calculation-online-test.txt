1-10. For the given inorganic chemical reaction
CH4 + Cl2   → CH3Cl + HCl
Molecular weights of the species CH4, Cl2, CH3Cl and HCl are 16, 71, 50.5 and 36.5 gms respectively.
1. For producing 1 mole of CH3Cl, how many moles of CH4 are required? (Consider Cl2 in excess)
a) 1
b) 4
c) 5
d) 6.5
a
2. For producing 1 mole of CH3Cl, how many moles of Cl2 are required? (Consider CH4 in excess)
a) 1
b) 4
c) 5
d) 6.5
a
3. For producing 1 mole of HCl, how many moles of CH4 are required? (Consider Cl2 in excess)
a) 1
b) 4
c) 5
d) 6.5
a
4. For producing 1 mole of HCl, how many moles of Cl2 are required? (Consider CH4 in excess)
a) 1
b) 4
c) 5
d) 6.5
a

c) Four times
