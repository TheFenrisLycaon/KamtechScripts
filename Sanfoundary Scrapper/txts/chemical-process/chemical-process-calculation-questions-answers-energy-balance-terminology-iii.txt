1. Everything outside a system boundary is
a) Surrounding
b) Boundary line
c) Open system
d) Closed system
a
2. The quantity of matter or region of space chosen for study is called
a) Boundary
b) Surrounding
c) System
d) None of the mentioned
c
3. System is enclosed by
a) Boundary
b) Surrounding
c) Layers
d) None of the mentioned
a

a) Transient state
b) Unsteady state
c) Both a and b
d) Neither a nor b
c
5. Unsteady state is also known as Transient state, the statement is
a) True
b) False
c) Applicable for some systems
d) None of the mentioned
a

a) Adiabatic system

b) Isobaric system

a) Adiabatic system
