1. Energy a system possesses because of its velocity relative to the surrounding is
a) Kinetic Energy
b) Potential Energy
c) Work
d) None of the mentioned
a
2. A system is at rest, the kinetic energy of the system is

b) Greater than zero
c) Less than zero
