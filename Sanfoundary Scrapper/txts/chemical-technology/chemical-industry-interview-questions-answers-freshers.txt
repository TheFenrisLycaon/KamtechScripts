1. What is the highest end use of urea?
a) solid fertilizer
b) liquid fertilizer
c) formaldehyde resin
d) melamine
a
2. Which process is associated with urea production?
a) High pressure stripping operation
b) Low temperature stripping operation
c) Low pressure stripping operation
d) Low temperature stripping operation
c
3. What is the undesirable product in urea production?
a) Ammonium carbonate
b) Biuret formation
c) Liquid NH3
d) Flakes formation
b
4. In which cycle NH3 and CO2 are used as raw materials?
a) Claude cycle
b) Wulff cycle
c) Cascades cycle
d) Montecatini solution recycle
d
5. In which process NH3 is compressed and returned to the autoclave?
a) Inventa process
b) Chemico process
c) Pechiney process
d) Montecatini process
b
6. What is the major engineering problem associated with ammonia production?
a) Corrosion
b) Heat recovery
c) Handling deposited carbon
d) Burner design
a
7. Which prevention can be taken to avoid the formation of biuret?
a) Temperature > melting point
b) Temperature < melting point
c) Temperature > boiling point
d) Temperature < boiling point
a
8. In which process unconverted NH3 is absorbed?
a) Stamicarbon process
b) Toyo koatsu process
c) Inventa process
d) Hot- recycle process
c
9. Which raw material used for the production of urea?
a) CO2+N2
b) CO2+NH3
c) NH2CONH2
d) NH3
b

a) Off-gases and NH3
