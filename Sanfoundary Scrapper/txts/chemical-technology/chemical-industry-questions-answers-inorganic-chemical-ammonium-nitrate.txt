1. Which of the following reaction unit is used in manufacture of Ammonium Nitrate by Stengel’s process?
a) Carbon steel
b) Graphite
c) Stainless steel
d) Tantalum
d
2. Constitution of which of the following element makes it highly explosive compound?
a) Ammonia
b) Nitrogen Oxide
c) Oxygen
d) Nitrogen Dioxide
b
3. Presence of which of the following enables ammonium nitrates to produce toxic fumes even after the initial fire is removed?
a) Bromides
b) Nitrates
c) Chlorides
d) Oxides
c
4. Which of the following material can be used to prevent decomposition of ammonium nitrate?
a) Ammonia
b) Chlorine
c) Nitrogen
d) Water
a
5. After drying and screening hot ammonium nitrate granules are treated with ___________
a) amines
b) chloride
c) oxides
d) water
a
6. Which component makes ammonium nitrate a good fertilizer?
a) Ammonia
b) Oxygen
c) Nitrate
d)  Hydrogen
c
7. What happens to the hot concentrated melt of ammonium nitrate after mixing of filling material?
a) Prilled
b) Fluidized bed granulation
c) Drum granulation
d) Granulation
a
8. Contamination with which of the following material does not have any harmful effect on ammonium nitrate?
a) Ammonia
b) Zinc
c) Oxygen
d) NO2
d
9. Ammonium nitrate is used as an intermediate in manufacture of which of the following?
a) Nitrolime
b) Urea
c) Ammonia
d) Nitric acid
a
10. Name the property of ammonium nitrate, which helps in storing and handling it.
a) Reducing nature
b) Oxidizing nature
c) Acidic
d) Alkaline
b
