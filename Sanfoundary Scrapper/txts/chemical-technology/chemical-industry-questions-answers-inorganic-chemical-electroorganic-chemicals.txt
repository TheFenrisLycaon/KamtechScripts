1. Steps involved in formation of adiponitrile are _______
a) Benzene → adipic acid → cyclohexane → adiponitrile
b) Cyclohexane → benzene → adipic acid → adiponitrile
c) Benzene → cyclohexane →adipic acid → adiponitrile
d) Cyclohexane → adipic acid → benzene → adiponitrile
c
2. Which of the following reaction takes place at cathode in electro chemical manufacture of adiponitrile from acrylonitrile?
a) H2O →2H++\(\frac{1}{2}\) O2+ 2e–
b) 2CH2=CHCN+2H2 O+2e–→NC – (CH2)4 – CN
c) 2CH2=CHCN+2H2 O+2e–→NC – (CH2)4 – CN+2OH–
d) 2CH2=CHCN+2H2 O→NC – (CH2)4 – CN+\(\frac{1}{2}\) O2
c
3. Cathode used in electro chemical manufacture of adiponitrile from acrylonitrile is ____________
a) Titanium
b) Platinum-coated carbon
c) Graphite
d) Lead
d
