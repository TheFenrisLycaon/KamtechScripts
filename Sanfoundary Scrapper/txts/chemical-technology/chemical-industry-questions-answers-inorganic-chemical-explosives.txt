1. Which one mentioned below is one of commercial explosives?
a) HMTD
b) Dynamite
c) RDX
d) C4
b
2. What is the property of low explosives?
a) Reaction rates are high
b) Requires an initiator
c) Reaction rates are slow
d) Yield at low flame temperature
c
3. In which categories explosives are used in highest amount?
a) Coal mining
b) Metal mining
c) Non-metallic mining
d) Construction
a
4. In which form C4 explosives are appeared?
a) Colourless crystal
b) Odorless
c) Smells like motor oil
d) Dyed pink powder
c
5. PETN ( Pentaerythritol tetranitrate) is highly sensitive for which factor?
a) Shock
b) Non-toxic
c) Insensitive to heat
d) Ignite and burn
a
6. Which explosives are only soluble in acetone but insoluble in water?
a) PETN
b) HMX
c) ANFO
d) C4
b
7. Which explosives is known as ‘octogen’?
a) RDX
b) HMX
c) Semtex
d) Black powder
b
8. Which explosives are used for primarily mines and demolition explosives?
a) TNT
b) Urea nitrate
c) HMTD
d) TATP
a
9. Which explosive corrodes most metal?
a) Smokeless powder
b) Black powder
c) HMTD
d) Dynamite
c
10. Which explosive is known as “magic marble of pardubice”?
a) ANFO
b) TNT
c) RDX
d) Semtex
d
