1. Which of the following statements regarding graphite are wrong?
a) Graphite is a semi-metal
b) Graphite is a form of coal
c) Graphite is used as heat resistant material
d) Graphite is an amorphous allotrope of carbon
d
2. Which of the following is raw material used in manufacture of graphite is burned out and requires replacement?
a) Graphitized grade coke
b) Saw dust
c) Furnacing sand
d) Pitch binder
b
3. What is the temperature at which coke is calcined in the manufacture of graphite?
a) 3000°C
b) 2000°C
c) 1400°C
d) 900°C
c
4. What does a run require in manufacture of graphite?
a) 15-20 days of heating
b) 2-4 days of cooling
c) 1 day to load and unload
d) 1 day each to load and unload
d
5. Charge forms the resistor in preparation of artificial graphite.
a) True
b) False
a
