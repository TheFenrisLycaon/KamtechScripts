1. What is the molecular weight of Oxygen?

b) 31
c) 32
d) 33

2. What is the molecular weight of Nitrogen?
a) 25
b) 26
c) 27
d) 28

3. Which of the mentioned purposes uses High purity oxygen?
a) Welding and cutting of metals
b) Open hearth steel purification
c) Medicinal purposes
d) All the mentioned
d
4. Which oxygen is used for blast furnace operations?
a) High purity oxygen
b) Low purity oxygen
c) Atmospheric oxygen
d) Liquid oxygen
b

a) N2
b) O2
c) H2
d) CO2
