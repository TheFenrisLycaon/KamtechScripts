1. Minerals are mined from all environments except from the ______________
a) ocean beaches
b) sub-sea floor rocks
c) continental shelves
d) deep sea floor
d
2. Oil and gas deposits are found in ________ region.
a) ocean beaches
b) sub-sea floor rocks
c) continental shelves
d) deep sea floor
b
3. Which separators are used to separate oversize pebbles and undersize clay particles if minerals such as monazite, zircon are present?
a) Revolving trommel
b) Shaking tables
c) Humphrey spirals
d) Magnetic and electrostatic separators
c
4. Which impurity of manganese nodules is leached using H2SO4 as solvent at temperature of 77°F?
a) Cobalt
b) Copper
c) Nickel
d) Iron
b
5. A differential leaching process is used for processing manganese nodules.
a) True
b) False
a
