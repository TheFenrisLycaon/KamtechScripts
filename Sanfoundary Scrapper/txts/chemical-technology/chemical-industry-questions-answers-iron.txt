1. Which among the following is not an iron ore?
a) Hematite
b) Magnetite
c) Siderite
d) Pyrrhotite
d
2. Which among the following is an essential chemical reaction for the manufacture of pig iron?
a) Fe2O3 + 3C → 2Fe + 3CO
b) FeS + C → Fe + CS
c) Fe3O4 + S → 3Fe + SO4
d) Fe2O3 + S → 2Fe + SO3
a
3. How can the production of pig iron be increased?
a) Increasing temperature decreasing pressure
b) Decreasing temperature
c) Increasing pressure
d) Decreasing pressure
c
4. What are the raw materials needed for the manufacture of pig iron?
a) Iron ore-Calcium-Coke
b) Iron ore-Coke
c) Iron ore-Calcium
d) Iron ore-Coke-Limestone
d
5. Coke consumption can be reduced in the manufacture of pig iron.
a) False
b) True
b
