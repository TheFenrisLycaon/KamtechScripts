1. Composition of ideal fiber required for paper making is _______________
a) High cellulose content, high lignin content
c) Low cellulose content, high lignin content
d) Low cellulose content, low lignin content
b
2. Which type of paper is used in producing cigarette paper product?
a) Wrapping paper
b) Book paper
c) Tissue paper
d) Writing paper
c
3. Fillers are added to reduce water penetration into paper products.
a) True
b) False
b
4. What is the capacity of single fourdrinier paper machine per day?
a) 50-100 tons
b) 400-500 tons
c) 1000-2000 tons
