1.  Which enzyme is used to hydrolyse starch to maltose to manufacture ethanol by fermentation?
a) Diastase
b) Alpha-Amylase
c) Lipase
d) Beta-Amylase
a
2. Which among the following methods cannot be used for the manufacture of ethanol?
a) From fermentation of molasses
b) From lignocelluloses biomass
c) Catalytic hydrogenation of ethylene
d) From microorganisms
d
3. What is mash made up of in the manufacture of ethanol from molasses?
a) Ammonium nitrate + Magnesium nitrate
b) Ammonium nitrate + Magnesium nitrate + Phosphorous
c) Ammonium sulphate + Magnesium sulphate
d) Ammonium sulphate + Magnesium sulphate + Phosphorous
d
4. Denatured ethanol is safe for drinking.
a) True
b) False
b
5. The pH of molasses solution in the manufacture of ethanol should be __________
a) 1-2
b) 2-3
c) 7-9
d) 4-5
d
