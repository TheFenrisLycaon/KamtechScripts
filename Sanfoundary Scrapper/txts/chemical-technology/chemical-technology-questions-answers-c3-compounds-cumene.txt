1. How is cumene manufactured?
a) Propylene alkylation of benzene
b) Catalytic hydrogenation of isopropanol
c) Oxidation of butane
d) Oxidation of propylene
a
2. How can we minimize the formation of polyalkylbenzene in the manufacture of cumene?
a) Using low benzene and propylene feed
b) Using high benzene and propylene feed
c) Using a high-end catalyst
d) Using a low-end catalyst
b
3. Which catalyst is used in the packed stage reactor column for the manufacture of cumene via propylene alkylation of benzene?
a) Silver
b) Copper
c) Palladium
d) H3PO4
d
4. Tubular reactor design is better than stage wise design the manufacture of cumene via propylene alkylation of benzene.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Which among the following is the molecular formula of cumene?
a) C9H12
b) C3H8O
c) CH3COOH
d) CH3CH2COOH
a
