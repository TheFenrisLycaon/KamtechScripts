1. Which among the following methods is not used for the manufacture of isoprene?
a) Isomerization of propylene dimer
b) Isobutylene formaldehyde reaction
c) Methyl ethyl ketone formaldehyde reaction
d) Ethylene cyanohydrin pyrolysis
d
2. Which catalyst is used in the manufacture of isoprene by isomerization of propylene dimer?
a) Triethyl aluminium
b) Trimethyl aluminium
c) Triphenyl aluminium
d) Tripropyl aluminium
d
3. Isoprene can be polymerized with a stereospecific catalyst. True or false?
a) True
b) False
a
4. What are the series of chemical reactions in manufacture of isoprene from isomerization of polypropylene dimer?
a) Dimerization-Isomerization-Pyrolysis
b) Pyrolysis-Isomerization-Dimerization
c) Dimerization-Pyrolysis-Isomerization
d) Pyrolysis-Dimerization-Isomerization
a
5. What kind of a catalyst is used in the packed bed reactor of isomerization section in the manufacture of isoprene from isomerization of polypropylene dimer?
a) Neutral type catalyst
b) Solid catalyst
c) Acid type catalyst
d) Gas type catalyst
c
