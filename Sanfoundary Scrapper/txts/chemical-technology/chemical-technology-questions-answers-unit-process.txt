1. Unit process involves changes in which of the mentioned?
a) Physical changes
b) Chemical Changes
c) Both Physical and chemical changes
d) None of the mentioned
b
2. Which of the mentioned is an example of Unit process?
a) Melting of ice
b) Boiling of water
c) Boiling of egg
d) Cooling of water
c
3. Ca(OH)2 + H2SO4 → CaSO4 + 2H2O is which type of reaction?
a) Replacement
b) Decomposition
c) Addition
d) Double decomposition
d
4. The reaction CaO + H2O → CH3CH2OH is an example of ________
a) Hydration
b) Dehydration
c) Hydrogenation
d) Dehydrogenation
a
5. Which of the mentioned come under Unit process?
a) Hydrolysis
b) Isomerization
c) Esterification
d) All the mentioned
d
