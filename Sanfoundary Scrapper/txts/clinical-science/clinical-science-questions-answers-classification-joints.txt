1. The number of cartilagineous structures in a long bone are ____________
a) 2
b) 5
c) 0
d) 1
a
2. The reason why people have joint pain in old age is because _____________
a) the bones become weak
b) the bones become too strong
c) the chondrocytes in articular cartilage become lesser in number
d) the blood supply to the joints reduces
c
3. Ellipsoid joint is found at __________
a) atlanto-occipital joint
b)  first carpo-metacarple joint
c) first tarso-metatarsle joint
d) atlando-axial joint.
a
4. Which of these is a multi-axial joint?
a) elbow joint
b)  ball and Socket joint
c) shoulder joint
d) thumb joint
b
5. Other name for condylar joint is ___________
a) Saddle joint
b) Plain joint
c) Ball and Socket Joint
d) Hinge Joint
d
6. In a synovial joint, the bones are connected to each other.
a) True
b) False
b
7. Synovial fluid secreted by synovial is rich in ___________
a) proteins
b) vitamins
c) minerals
d) fats
d
8. Acromioclavicular joint is an example of   ___________
a) Ball and Socket Joint
b) Hinge Joint
c) Plain Joint
d) Pivot Joint
c
9. The tooth like structure of C2 vertebra that forms Pivot Joint with C1 vertebra is called as odontoint. The other name is ____________
a) flagella
b) dens
c) matrix
d) condyl
b
