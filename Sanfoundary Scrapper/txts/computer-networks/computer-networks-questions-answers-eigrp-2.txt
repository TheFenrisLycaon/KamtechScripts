1. EIGRP is a routing protocol design by Cisco.
a) True
b) False
a
2. EIGRP metric is ________
a) K-values
b) Bandwidth only
c) Hop Count
d) Delay only
a

a) VLSM/subnetting
b) Auto summary
c) Unequal cast load balancing
d) All of the mentioned
d

a) 5 seconds (LAN), 60 seconds (WAN)
b) 5 seconds (LAN), 5 seconds (WAN)
c) 15s
d) 180s

a) 90
b) 170
c) 110
d) 91

c) 110
d) 100

b) DUAL
c) Linkstat
