1. In a dry environment, concrete strength will be loosed as much as __________ % in moist environment.
a) 30
b) 40
c) 50
d) 60
c
2. After finishing concrete surface must be kept __________
a) Dry
b) First dry it and then wet it
c) First wet it and then dry it
d) Wet
d
3. A ___________ paste structure undergoes ___________ creep.
a) Good, high
b) Poor, high
c) Good, average
d) Poor, low
b
4. SCC has __________ yield stress.
a) Low
b) High
c) Medium
d) 75%
a
5. SCC has __________ deformability.
a) Low
b) High
c) Medium
d) 75%
b
6. SCC has __________ viscosity.
a) Low
b) High
c) Medium
d) 75%
c
7. SCC has ___________ segregation resistance.
a) Good
b) Bad
c) Average
d) Poor
a
8. SCC is measured using the _________
a) Picat’s apparatus
b) Slump-flow test
c) Slump test
d) Standard Consistency Test
