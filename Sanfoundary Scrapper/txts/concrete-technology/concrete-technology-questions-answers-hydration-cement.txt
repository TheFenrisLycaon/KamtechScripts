1. What is hydration of cement?
a) Chemical reaction of cement with acid
b) Chemical reaction of cement with water
c) Chemical reaction of cement with base
d) Chemical reaction of cement with salt, and acid
b
2. As the hydration proceeds the deposits of hydrated products on the original cement grains makes the diffusion of water to unhydrated nucleus ________
a) Easy
b) Very difficult
c) Very easy
d) Average
b
3. The development of first 28 days strength is on account of the hydration of __________
a) C2S
b) C3S
c) C3A
d) C4AF
b
4. Which compound is liberates higher heat?
a) C2S
b) C3S
c) C3A
d) C4AF
b
5. Which compound is liberates lower heat?
a) C2S
b) C3S
c) C3A
d) C4AF
a
6. Which compound may lead to a rapid stiffening of the paste with a large amount of the heat generation?
a) C2S
b) C3S
c) C3A
d) C4AF
c
7. In order to prevent this rapid reaction _______ is added to the clinker.
a) C4AF
b) Gypsum
c) Water
d) Extra cement
b
