1. Loss on Ignition (L.O.I.) is the loss in weight of cement after being heated to ____________
a) 1000°C
b) 100°C
c) 500°C
d) 1500°C
a
2. On cooling below 1250oC, C3S decomposes ____________
a) Fast
b) Slowly
c) Never
d) Depends on the conditions
b
3. If the moisture content of 5 to 10% by weight, then the bulking of sand is increased by __________
a) 20
b) 30
c) 40
d) 50
d
4. When sand is fully dry then it’s volume is _________
a) Equal
b) Less
c) More
d) Can’t say
a
5. The mineral oil if present in mixing for concrete ______________
a) Improves strength
b) Reduces strength
c) Gives more slump
d) Gives a smooth surface
a
6. To neutralize 200 ml of sample should not require more than 10 ml of __________ normal HCI using methyl orange.
a) 1
b) .1
c) 10
d) .01
b
7. The loss in weight should not exceed ________ % when tested with Na2SO4 and ________ % with MgSO4 solution.
a) 12, 18
b) 18, 12
c) 10, 15
d) 15, 10
a
