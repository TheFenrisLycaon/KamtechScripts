1. What is the moisture content in slurry for wet process?
a) 35-50%
b) 12%
c) 40-45%
d) 100%
a
2. The slurry, in its movement down the kiln, encounters a progressively higher temperature. At first, the water is driven off and __________ is liberated.
a) SiO2
b) CO2
c) Gypsum
d) CaO
b
3. For complete hydration of cement the w/c ratio needed is ________
a) More than 0.25
b) More than 0.25 but less than 0.35
c) More than 0.35 but less than 0.60
d) More than 0.60
c
4. The minimum water to cement ratio for cement concrete to hydrate is _______
a) 0.65
b) 0.5
c) 0.38
d) 0.27
c
5. Approximate percentage range of A2O3 in OPC is _______
a) 17-25
b) 3-8
c) 3- 10
d) 4-15
c
6. What is the percentage of iron oxide in white cement?
a) High
b) Very high
c) Medium
d) Very low
d
7. What is the percentage of air by volume in Air Entraining Cement?
a) 3-4
b) 10-11
c) 7-8
d) 9
a
