1.  If the events in post transcriptional modification are listed as-
       i) Polyadenylation   ii) capping   iii) splicing
What would be the correct sequence?
a) i->ii->iii
b) iii->ii->i
c) i->iii->ii
d)ii->i->iii
d

a) Pol II transcripts
b) Pol I and Pol III transcripts
c) Pol I transcripts

d

a) ADP
