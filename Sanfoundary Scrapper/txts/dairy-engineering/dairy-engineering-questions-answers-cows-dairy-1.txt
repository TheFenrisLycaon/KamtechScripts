1. What is the most common breed of dairy cow?
a) Holstein
b) Aryshire
c) Brown Swiss
d) Jersey
a
2. What is the cow’s first milk called?
a) Holostrum
b) Drostrum
c) Colostrum
d) milk
c
3. What are the six nutrients animals need?
I. carbohydrate
II. fat and protein
III. vitamin
IV. mineral and water
a) I only
b) II and III
c) III and IV
d) I, II, III, and IV
d
4. How soon before the show should an exhibitor begin fitting and training animals?
a) 5months
b) 2 months
c) 1 year
d) 8months

a) cow
b) vache
c) moose
d) bull

a) omnivore
b) autotrophs
c) carnivore
d) herbivore

I. Holstein
II. Guernsey
III. Jersey

b) II only
c) III only
d) I, II, III
d
8. What special ingredient in colostrums helps fight infection and disease in the newborn calf?
a) protein
b) carbohydrate
c) ammonia
d) antibodies
d
9. What is the nutrient that eventually becomes part of muscle, bone and blood?
a) protein
b) carbohydrate
c) mineral
d) antibodies
a
10. What purpose is served by covering a dairy show animal with a blanket?
I. loosens the hair
II. keeps animal clean
a) I only
b) II only
c) I and II
d) None
c

a) reproduction period
b) gestation period
c) feed period
d) pregnancy

a) Holstein
b) Brown Swiss
c) Arya
d) jersey
a

a) grey and white
b) brown and black

a) freezing
b) chilling
c) pasteurization
d) vacuum treatment

a) vitamin D
b) vitamin C
