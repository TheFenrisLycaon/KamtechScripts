1. Which of the following operation has to be performed to increase the sampling rate by an integer factor I?

4. If X(z) is the z-transform of x(n), then what is the z-transform of interpolated signal v(m)?
a) X(zI)
b) X(z+I)
c) X(z/I)
d) X(zI)
d
V(z)=\(\sum_{m=-∞}^∞ v(m)z^{-m}\) 
=\(\sum_{m=-∞}^∞ x(m)z^{-mI}\) 
= X(z-I)
