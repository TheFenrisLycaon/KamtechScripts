1.  When the liquid becomes dispersed and gas becomes continuous, such process is called?
a) Spray regime
b) Emulsion regime
c)  Pray Regime

a) Entrainment and Velocity
b) Gas and flooding velocity
c)  Vapor and liquid rate

a) Design vapor to operable throughput

b) Tray spacing
