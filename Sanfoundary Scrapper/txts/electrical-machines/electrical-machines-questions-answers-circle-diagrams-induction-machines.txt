3. A 10kW, 50 Hz, 3-phase induction motor develops the rated torque at 1440rpm. If the load torque is reduced to half, then the motor speed is?
a) 1470rpm
b) 1410rpm
c) 1400rpm
d) 1444rpm
a
             T2/T1 = s2/s1
			 s2 = (1/2)*0.04 = 0.02
			 Nr = (1-0.02)*1500 = 1470 rpm.
