1.	The skin effect is a phenomenon observed in
a)	Insulators
b)	Dielectrics
c)	Conductors
d)	Semiconductors
c
2.	The skin depth is measured in
a)	Meter
b)	Millimetre
c)	Centimetre
d)	Micrometer
d
3.	The skin depth is calculated from the amplitude of the wave. State true/false
a)	True
b)	False
a

a)	0.5

c)	2
d)	4
