1. Which of the following designs do not require the parabolic curve?
a) Light reflectors
b) Sound reflectors
c) Cooling towers
d) Arches
c
2. Which of the following design requires the parabolic curves?
a) Cooling towers
b) Water channels
c) Stuffing box
d) Light reflectors
d
3. Which of the following equations belongs to the parabolic curve?
a) Y2+X2 = -1
b) Y = X
c) Y = 3(X2-1)
d) XY = 1
c
4. The focus point of the parabolic equation Y = 3(X2-1) is ______
a) (1, 3)
b) (⅓, 1)
c) (0, 1/12-3)
d) (1/12+3, 0)
c
5. The directrix of the parabolic equation Y = 3(X2-1) is ______
a)  Y = -37/12
b) Y = 3
c) Y = 1
d) Y = -3/14
a

a) 1
b) ½
c) 3/2

7. The standard form of parabolic curve is (X-h)2 = 4p(Y-k).
a) True
b) False
a
8. Which of the following method is used for construction parabolic curves?
a) Rectangular method
b) Arc of the circle method
c) Concentric circle method
d) Oblong method

9. Which of the following is used for the construction of ellipse?
a) Rectangular method
b) Parallelogram method
c) Concentric circle method
d) Tangent method
c
10. The length of the latus rectum is four times the focal length for the parabolic curve.
a) True
b) False
a
11. The opening of the parabola for the equation Y2 = 4ax, is on _______
a) The negative side of X-axis
b) The positive side of X-axis
c) The negative side of Y-axis
d) The positive side of Y-axis
b
12. The opening of the parabola for the equation Y2 = -4ax, is on the _______
a) The negative side of X-axis
b) The positive side of X-axis
c) The negative side of Y-axis
d) The positive side of Y-axis
a
13. The opening of the parabola for the equation X2 = 4aY, is on the _______
a) The negative side of X-axis
b) The positive side of X-axis
c) The negative side of Y-axis
d) The positive side of Y-axis
d
14. The opening of the parabola for the equation X2 = -4aY, is on the _______
a) The negative side of X-axis
b) The positive side of X-axis
c) The negative side of Y-axis
d) The positive side of Y-axis

a) (0, 0)
b) (a/2, 0)

d) (0, a/2)
