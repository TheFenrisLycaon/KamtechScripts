1. When the axis of a solid is parallel to a plane, the projection of the solid shows the true shape and size of its base.
a) False
b) True
a
2. When the axis of a solid perpendicular to H.P., __________ should be drawn first.
a) top view
b) front view
c) side view
d) rare view
a
3. When the axis of a solid perpendicular to V.P., __________ should be drawn first.
a) top view
b) front view
c) side view
d) rare view
b
4. When the axis of a solid parallel to H.P and V.P., __________ should be drawn first.
a) top view
b) front view
c) side view
d) rare view
c

5. While drawing a projection, If the first top view is drawn and then the front view is projected from the top view. Then the axis of the solid is _____________
