1. What is the maximum permissible amount of ash on carbonization after coal is washed?
a) 2%
b) 6%

2. What is the solid residue that remains after heating of coal in the absence of air?
a) Resin
b) Coke
c) Powder
d) Whisker
b

a) 10-20%
b) 25-40%
c) 50-60%
d) 70-80%
d
4. For how long must coke be burnt?
a) 2-3 hours
b) 6-8 hours
c) 18-24 hours
d) 30-36 hours
d
5. What is the appearance of oven coke?
a) Bluish-green
b) Reddish-brown
c) Dark grey to light silver

a) Purity
b) Porosity
c) Low smoke

a) 100-250oC
b) 250-400oC

a) Ironmaking
b) Production of gas
c) Fuel briquettes
d) Fishnets

a) Phenol formaldehyde
b) Coumarone-indene

b) Paint
c) Thermal insulation
d) Ion exchange

13. What is Portland cement primarily composed of?
a) SiO2
b) CaO
c) Fe2O3

a) 950oC
b) 1250oC

15. The hardening process of Portland cement is known as _______
a) Calcination
b) Oxidation
c) Hydration

a) 3% baryte
b) 3% anhydrite
c) 3% hanksite

a) 3.15
b) 2.94
