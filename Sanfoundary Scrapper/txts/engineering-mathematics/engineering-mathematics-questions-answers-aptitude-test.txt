1. If f(t) = sinhat, then its Laplace transform is?
a) eat
b) s ⁄ s2-a2
c) a ⁄ s2-a2
d) Exists only if ‘t’ is complex
c
\(\{f(t)\}=F(s)=\int_0^{\infty} f(t)e^{-st}dt\) 
put f(t) = sinhat
On solving, a ⁄ s2-a2 is obtained.
