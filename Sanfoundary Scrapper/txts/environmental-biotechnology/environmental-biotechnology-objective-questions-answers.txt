1. Sugar containing agricultural products produce alcohol with the help of ___________

b) distilleries
c) ocean
d) rivers
b
2. Spirits are diluted alcohol.
a) True
b) False

3. Which of the following industries doesn’t use alcohol in their respective industries?
a) Sugar Industry
b) Vinegar production

4. _____________ is added to the spirits to enhance it.
a) flavor additives
b) acid
c) alcohol
d) water
a
5. Which of the following steps in not followed during the production process?

b) Exhaust vapor condensate
