1. Which of the following phases doesn’t lead to bio accumulation in aquatic organism?
a) Accumulation phase
b) Steady-state
c) Elimination phase
d) Death phase
d
2. The initial phase of uptake of toxicant is very fast.
a) True
b) False
a
3. The toxicant accumulation can occur by direct absorption from the medium and ________
a) Toxification
b) Food chain transfer
c) Biochemical degradation
d) Potassium
b
4. In young organism, steady state can be achieved very fast.
a) True
b) False
a
5. The time to reach half of the concentration of the toxicant is a function of environmental concentration and _______________
a) Membrane permeability rate
b) Partial coefficient
c) Rate of active transport
d) Rate of active transport
b
6. Which of the following factor doesn’t influence the rate of Bioaccumulation by food?
a) Temperature
b) Pressure
c) Food type
d) Size of animal
b
7. At plateau of bio accumulation, rate of uptake is equal to rate of elimination.
a) True
b) False
a
8. Sometimes organism metabolizes the accumulated toxicant to facilitate its own growth.
a) True
b) False
a
9. What does ∆t depicts in the given formula: C = Cmax Δt/B + Δt?
a) t-t0
b) t0-t
c) C-C0
d) C0-C
a
t=Exposure end time
t0=Exposure beginning time C=0
C=Concentration of xenobiotic in organism, at steady-state
B=The initial concentration of the xenobiotic present in the organism at the time of exposure.
