1. Acidic soils can be characterized by _______
a) Low pH
b) High pH
c) Texture
d) Colour
a
2. Which of the following ions is not found in excess in acidic soils?
a) Al
b) Fe
c) Mg
d) Cu
c
3. Which of the following ion is not found in acidic soils?
a) Ca
b) Al
c) Fe
d) Cu
a
4. Bioconcentration factor is the concentration of a pollutant in a organism compared to that present in the environment.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Which of the following is majorly found in acid sulphate soils?
a) Pyrite
b) Copper
c) Aluminium
d) Magnesium

b) False
