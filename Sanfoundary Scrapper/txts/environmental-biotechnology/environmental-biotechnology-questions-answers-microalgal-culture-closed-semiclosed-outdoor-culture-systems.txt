1. Which of the following can utilize the light source to promote growth of phototrophic microorganisms?
a) Algal blooms
b) Photo bioreactor
c) Stir tank bioreactor
d) Zooplankton
b
2. Which of the following is an important step before further processing of the algal biomass grown in a photo bioreactor?
a) Harvesting
b) Centrifugation
c) pH change
d) Volume change
a
3. How does the oil from the algal biomass is removed?
a) Solvent extraction
b) Water extraction
c) Gel extraction
d) Sieving
a
4. Which of the following organisms is not used in photo bioreactor?
a) Mosses
b) Microalgae
c) Purple bacteria
d) E. coli
d
5. Water parameters change after being used in various industries, which of the following parameters is not altered in the end?
a) Physical
b) Chemical
c) pH
d) Volume
d
6. What happens when the untreated water gets into the surface water which causes deterioration of water quality?
a) Volume
b) Biological characteristics
c) Temperature
d) Lipophilicity
b
7. The variety of life forms gets affected due to untreated waste water.
a) True
b) False
a
8. Which of the following is not a result of the transformed primary productivity of water bodies?
a) Algal blooms
b) Adverse health effects
c) Reduction in water quality
d) Increase in water quality
d
9. The secondary pollution is caused due physical and chemical purification techniques.
a) True
b) False
a
10. Which of the following techniques is environment friendly and cost effective?
a) Physical
b) Chemical
c) Biological
d) Combination of physical and chemical
c
11. The decomposition of the organic compounds leads to conversion of released minerals into usable biomass.
a) True
b) False
a
