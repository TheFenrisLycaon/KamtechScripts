1. ____________ process is the main pathway for degradation of bioorganic compounds.
a) catabolic
b) anabolic
c) catalytic
d) gluconeogenesis
a
2. Anabolic processes of microorganisms help in the decomposition of bioorganic or organic compounds.
a) True
b) False
b
3. __________ of some xenobiotic can be traced back to a biological origin.
a) Carbon portion
b) Sugar portion
c) Potassium portion
d) Calcium portion
a
4. Which of the following return to the carbon cycle after incineration?
a) Coal
b) Oil
c) Natural gas
d) water
d
5. Stripping of carbon dioxide is necessary to prevent a drop in pH and _______
a) exhaust vapor condensate
b) fermentation
c) distillation
d) remove heat energy
d
