1. Technique which is used to re-mediate contamination above the water table is called as Bioluminescence.
a) True
b) False
b
2. Which of the following is not a principal approach under ex-situ techniques?
a) Soil banking
b) Soil slurry bioreactors
c) Land farming
d) Bio-sparging
d
3. ______________ technique is usually done within the earthwork banking that has been constructed.
a) Land farming
b) Bio sparging
c) Bio-accumulation
d) Bio-degradation
a
4. Land farming is a type of low tech bio reactor.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Which of the following process is not involved in the pretreatment stage of land farming?
a) Soil excavation
b) Soil screening
c) Storage
d) Bio-magnification
d
6. Land farming processes usually occurs in ____________
a) Lined earthworks
b) Rapid earthworks
c) DLAS architecture
d) Valley landscape
a

a) Low density lipoprotein
b) High density lipoprotein
c) Low density polyethylene liner
d) High density polyethylene (HDPE) liner
d

a) Oxidation of sodium thiosulphate
b) Methanogenesis

d) Acetogenesis
