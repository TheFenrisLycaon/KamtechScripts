1. Process integration is not achieved by which among the following?
a) In situ or ex situ
b) Extensive or intensive
c) Biological
d) Physical
d
2. Pick-and-mix approach makes the process integration a flexible approach in cleaning up the land.
a) True
b) False
a
3. Which among the following processes doesn’t come under phyto-remediation?
a) Bio-accumulation
b) Concentration and excavation
c) Phytoextraction
d) Phytostabilisation
b
4. Which of the following is the most reliable method of soil remediation?
a) Bio-magnification
b) Bio-remediation
c) Lyophilization
d) Chemical method
b
5.  ____________ can be used to degrade typical contaminants using carbon source for their growth.
a) Microbes
b) Chemicals
c) Quorum quenching
d) Lyophilization
a
6. Which among the following doesn’t have higher potential to soil remediation?
a) Bacteria
b) Plants
c) Fungi
d) Pathogens
d
7. Which of the following processes uses hydroponics to remove water contaminants?
a) Rhizofiltration
b) Pollution control
c) Antibiotic development
d) Manufacturing process
a
8. The process by which microorganisms utilize contaminants as their food sources thereby degrading them is called as ____________
a) Inorganic compounds
b) Isolated bio-components
c) Toxic compounds
d) Mineralisation
d
9. Decomposition of remains from mineralization process is done by ______
a) Other microorganisms
b) Entering the food chain
c) Increasing dissolved oxygen
d) Producing methane gas
a
10. Co-metabolism is a process in which contaminants is again taken up by the microorganisms and is used as a food source.
a) True
b) False
b
11. Immobilization is used to remove which type of contaminants?
a) Hydrogen
b) Nitrogen
c) Carbon monoxide
d) Metals
d
12. Which among the following is a process of immobilization?
a) Bio-accumulation
b) Bio-remediation
c) Bio-degradation
d) Bio-magnification
a
13. Bio-remediation is most suitable for the degradation of __________
a) Methane
b) Nitrogen
c) Organic chemicals
d) Carbon dioxide
c
14. Bio-remediation can also be used to degrade __________
a) Methane
b) Nitrogen
c) Radionuclides
d) Carbon dioxide
c
15. Which among the following is not suitable for treatment by bio-remediation?
a) Hdrocarbons
b) Fungicides
c) Surfactants
d) Multiple sclerosis
d
