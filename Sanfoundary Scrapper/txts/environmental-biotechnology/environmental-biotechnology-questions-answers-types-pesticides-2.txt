1. Which of the following is a flower derived insecticide?
a) Pyrethroid insecticide
b) Acaricides
c) Nematicides
d) Fungicides
a
2. In Pyrethroids, which of the following channel is targeted for imposing toxicity?
a) Sodium
b) Calcium
c) Potassium
d) Iron
a
3. Which of the following is used to prevent germination of fungal spores?
a) Fungicides
b) Insecticides
c) Nematicides
d) Acaricides
a
4. Eradicants group of Fungicides remove the infection totally.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Which of the following is not used for protection of plants against fungus before the development of synthetic fungicides?
a) Sulphur compound
b) Copper compound
c) Lime sulphur
d) Uranium
d
6. Which of the following pesticide degradation methods is harmless for the environment?
a) Microorganisms
b) Chemical methods
c) Physical methods
d) Invertebrates
