1. Which of the following constituent is absent in beer?
a) Alcohol
b) Carbonic acid
c) Water
d) Cream
d
2. Which the following is not a kind of beer?
a) Lager
b) Top fermented
c) Bottom fermented
d) Medium fermented
d
3. Which of the following operation steps is not followed after the malting step?
a) Wort production
b) Fermentation
c) Storing
d) Cleaning
d
4. Starch in the malt is converted into fermentable sugar prior to fermentation step.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Which of the following unit operations does not generate waste water?
a) Brewing
b) Fermentation
c) Packaging
d) Malting
d
6. Which of the following measures is usually lower in brewery wastewater?
a) BOD
b) Temperature
c) pH
d) Turbidity
a
7. Alkaline pH values are usually observed in the bottle washing waste water.
a) True
b) False
a
8. What of the following is removed during the pre-treatment process?
a) Settable solids
b) Supernatant
c) Homogenization
d) Salting
a
9. Biological neutralization of alkalis is usually done during the ___________
a) Floatation and fat separation
b) Mixing
c) BOD degradation
d) Pasteurization
c
10. Reactors that run without heating of wastewater, are usually used.
a) True
b) False
a
