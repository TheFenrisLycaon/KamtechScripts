1. ______ zone lies between Euphotic and Benthic zone.
a) Euphotic zone
b) Benthic zone
c) Littoral zone
d) Climate zone
c
2. The uppermost zone in lakes is called as ____________
a) Euphotic zone
b) Benthic zone
c) Littoral zone
d) Climate zone
a
3. _______ is the process of overgrowth of plants and algae in lakes.
a) Transpiration
b) Reproduction
c) Eutrophication
d) Photosynthesis
c
4. Which of the following promotes eutrophication of lakes?
a) Magnesium
b) Sodium
c) Calcium
d) Phosphorous
d
5. In which zone, decomposition of the dead organism takes place?
a) Climate zone
b) Benthic zone
c) Littoral zone
d) Euphotic zone
b
6. Which one of the following lakes has minimum productivity?
a) Mesotrophic lake
b) Senescent lake
c) Oligotrophic lake
d) Eutrophic lake
c
7. ________ is the process of acceleration of eutrophication due to increased phosphorous content in lakes.
a) Photosynthesis
b) Chemical eutrophication
c) Cultural eutrophication
d) Custom eutrophication
c
8. The eutrophication process leads to turbidity in lakes.
a) True
b) False
a
9. Which type of pollution is Cultural eutrophication?
a) Water pollution
b) Noise pollution
c) Thermal pollution
d) Soil pollution
a
10. The lakes are classified into how many types on the basis of productivity?
a) 2
b) 3
c) 4
d) 6
c
11. The depth of Euphotic zone is measured by ____________
a) Calorimeter
b) Turbidity meter
c) Secchi disk
d) Radar
c
