1. The flow of sewage in the sewers is independent of its slope and geometry.
a) True
b) False
b
2. The expression of the Chezy’s formula is given by ____________
a) V = C/RS
b) V = CRS2
c) V = C (RS)1/2
d) V = C/ (RS)1/2
c
3. The value of Kutter’s coefficient for cast iron is ____________
a) 0.02
b) 0.012
c) 0.12
d) 0.13
b
4. The value of manning’s coefficient for cement concrete is ____________
a) 0.017
b) 0.13
c) 0.013
d) 0.17
c
5. Which of the following conduit material has the maximum Kutter’s coefficient in terms of good surface?
a) Brick
b) Salt glazed stoneware
c) Cast iron
d) Galvanized iron
a
6. The value of Bazen’s constant for good earthen channels is ____________
a) 0.12
b) 1.54
c) 0.50
d) 3.17
c
7. Which of the following has a minimum manning’s coefficient in terms of fair interior surface?
a) Plastic smooths
b) Asbestos cement
c) Brick
d) Cement concrete
a
8. Which of the following is the expression of  the Manning’s formula?
a) V = R1/3 S1/2/ N
b) V = R2/3 S2/ N
c) V = R2/3 S1/2/ N
d) V = R2/3 S1/2
c
9. Which of the following order is correct regarding Hazen and William’s coefficient?
i. Steel pipe
ii. Cement lined pipe
iii. Wood stave pipe
iv. Asbestos cement pipe
a) i > ii > iii > iv
b) i > iii > ii > iv
c) iv > ii > iii > i
d) iv > iii > ii > i
d
