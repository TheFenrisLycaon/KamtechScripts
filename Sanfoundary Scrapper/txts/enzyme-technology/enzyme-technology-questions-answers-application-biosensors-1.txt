1. Biosensors have been applied in medical field to __________
a) measure BOD
b) detect toxic compounds
c) detect plant neutrients
d) diagnose infectious diseases
d
• Agriculture: To detect plant nutrients, fertilizers and pesticides.
• Environment: For pollution monitoring – BOD, COD, NH3, NO3, SO3 etc.
• Mining: To detect toxic compounds and gases.
2. Pick odd one out.
a) BOD
b) COD
c) Mustard gas
d) NH3
