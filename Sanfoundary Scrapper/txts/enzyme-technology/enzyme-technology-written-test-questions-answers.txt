1. In agriculture, biosensors have not been used for which of the following?
a) Detection of plant neutrients
b) Detect fertilizers
c) Detect toxic compounds
d) Detection pesticides
c
• Detect plant neutrients.
• Detect fertilizers.
• Detect pesticides.
Biosensors are used in the mining industry to detect toxic compounds and gases.
2. Biosensors are used in food industry to _________
a) sense taste
b) detect mustard gas
c) detect acid alcohols
d) determine fatigue
a
• Defense: To detect mustard gas, nerve gas, etc.
• Industry: To detect alcohol, acids, phenols, etc.
• Sports: To determine fatigue by measuring levels of lactic acid, ammonia in perspiration.
3. Glucose level as low as 0.15 mmol can be detected by using _________ enzyme.
a) penicillinase
b) putrescine oxidase
c) glucose oxidase
d) alcohol oxidase
c
4. To detect the freshness of meat of fish, which of the following enzyme is not required?
a) ATPase
b) Amino oxidase
c) Putrescine oxidase
d) Invertase muta-rotase
d
5. _____________ measures protein interaction in real time without any labelling technique.
a) Calorimetric biosensor
b) Surface Plasmon Resonance biosensor
c) Penicillin measuring biosensor
d) Portable biosensor
b
6. Pick the odd one out.
a) Acid
b) Fatigue
c) Alcohol
d) Phenol
