1. Which of the following describes the displacement or swept volume?
a) Vs=\(\frac{Π}{4}\)∗d2l
b) Vs=\(\frac{Π}{16}\)∗d2l
c) Vs=\(\frac{Π}{32}\)∗d2l
d) Vs=\(\frac{Π}{4}\)∗d3l
a
2. Which of these is not an external combustion engine?
a) Beam engines
b) Steam engines
c) Stirling engines
d) Diesel engines
d
3. Relative efficiency is the ratio of _________
a) \(\frac{actual \, thermal \, efficiency}{mechanical \, efficiency} \) 
b) \(\frac{actual \, thermal \, efficiency}{air-standard \, efficiency} \) 
c) \(\frac{air-standard \, efficiency}{actual \, thermal \, efficiency} \) 
d) \(\frac{mechanical \, efficiency}{actual \, thermal \, efficiency} \) 
b
Relative efficiency = \(\frac{actual \, thermal \, efficiency}{air-standard \, efficiency} \)

a) PbO5 + Pb + 4H2SO4 = 4PbSO2 + 4H20
b) 2PbO2 + 2H2SO4 = 2PbO + 2SO4 + 2H2O + O2
c) PbO2 + Pb + 2H2SO4 = 2PbSO4 + 2H2O
