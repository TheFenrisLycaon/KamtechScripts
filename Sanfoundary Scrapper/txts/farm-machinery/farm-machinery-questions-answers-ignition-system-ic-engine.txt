1. Which device is used for interrupting the low voltage primary current in the battery ignition?
a) Dynamo
b) Storage battery
c) Condenser
d) Distributor
d
2. What is the name of the chemical solution used in a battery for chemical reactions?
a) Container
b) Terminal wires
c) Electrolyte
d) Separators
c
3. What should be the minimum specific gravity after which the electrolyte should be charged?
a) 1.225
b) 1.335
c) 2.334
d) 1.123
a
4. How much spark per minute is required by an eight-cylinder engine running at 4000 rpm?
a) 10000
b) 12000
c) 16000
d) 20000
c
5. What are the types of spark plug?
a) Cold plug
b) Hot plug
c) Hot and Cold plug
d) Plug
c
6. What percent of battery loss in 24 hours is known as battery leakage?
a) 2%
b) 3%
c) 1%
d) 1.5%
c
7. Which device is used to generate an electric current for producing spark in I.C. engine?
a) Magneto
b) Dynamo
c) Condenser
d) Plates
a
8. The process of igniting fuel by heat of compression is called?
a) Cooling ignition method
b) Compression ignition method
c) Compression initial method
d) Specific gravity method
b
9. Which device is used to control the quantity of oil circulation and to maintain correct pressure in the lubricating system?
a) Relief valve
b) Grease
c) Crankcase breather
d) By pass filter
a
