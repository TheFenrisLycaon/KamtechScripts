1. Who edited the second edition of ‘Brewing Microbiology’?
a) Rainbow
b) Priest and Campbell
c) Dolezil and Kirsop
d) Fernandez and Simpson
b
2. ‘The Beer is resistant to the microbial spoilage’. Who noticed the phenomena?
a) Fernandez
b) Simpson
c) Rainbow
d) Priest
c
3. Who correlated the resistance of spoilage to the beer parameters?
a) Rainbow
b) Priest and Campbell
c) Dolezil and Kirsop
d) Fernandez and Simpson
d

a) Ouchterlony test
b) RPR test
c) Catalase test
d) Coombs test
c
5. Who devised Gram’s test?
a) Hans Christian
b) Rainbow
c) Fernandez
d) Jacob
a

a) Acetobacter aceti
b) Escherichia coli
c) Gluconobacter oxydans
d) Streptococcus pneumoniae
d

a) Methylene blue
b) Safranin
c) Phenolphthalein
d) Methyl orange
b
8. Who gave the explanation for hop resistance?
a) Hammond
b) Simpson
c) Sami
d) Fernandez
c
9. Which of the following is not reclassified into Saccharomyces cerevisiae?
a) S. tubidans
b) S. willianus
c) S. diastaticus
d) S. bayanus
d

a) Endotoxins
b) Exotoxins
c) Neurotoxins
d) Enterotoxins
b

a) Sources
b) Nitrosamines
c) Biofilms
d) Biogenic Amines
c

a) Dental plaque
b) Slimes
c) Biofouling
d) Biogenic Amines
