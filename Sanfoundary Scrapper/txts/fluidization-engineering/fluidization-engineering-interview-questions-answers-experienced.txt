1. Why was fluidized bed not used commercially for production of gasoline from natural gas?
a) Conversion was below par
b) Scale-up was difficult
c) Back-mixing
d) All of the mentioned
d
2. Why was conversion in the production of gasoline in fluidized bed was less?
a) Back-mixing and by-passing
b) Recycling
c) Regeneration
d) None of the mentioned
a
3. What is the main reason for lower efficiencies in the commercial operations of synthetic reactions through fluidized beds?
a) Gas bypassing
b) Backing mixing
c) Bubble behaviour
d) All of the mentioned
d
4. Why are electrodes placed in resistance-heated fluidized bed?
a) Achieve high temperatures
b) Acts as heat transfer surface
c) Helps in magnetizing the metallic particles
d) None of the mentioned
a
5. Operating conditions and feed ratios are different for all these processes, however, conversion of limiting reagent, acetic acid is roughly 50-70%, true or false?
a) True
b) False

6. In the dilute phase reacting system, reacting gases carry suspended catalyst through tubes of a vertical heat exchanger with velocity of?
a) 3 to 12 m/s
b) 12 to 24 m/s
c) 1 to 3 m/s
