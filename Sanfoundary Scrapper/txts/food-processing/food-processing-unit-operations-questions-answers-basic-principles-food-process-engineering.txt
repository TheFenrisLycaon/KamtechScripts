1. The basic operations done in the food process engineering are called as __________
a) unit operations
b) processing
c) process management
d) unit process
a
2. Which of the following are the unit operations?
a) Distillation, evaporation, centrifugation
b) Distillation, membrane separation, sampling
c) Membrane separation, centrifugation, sampling
d) Evaporation, centrifugation, sampling
a
3. Unit operation is based on ______________
a) law of conservation of mass only
b) law of conservation of energy  only
c) law of conservation of mass and energy
d) third law of thermodynamics only
c
4. Are these statements about the contact equilibrium processes true?
Statement 1: Gas absorption, crystallization comes under the category of contact equilibrium processes.
Statement 2: Contact equilibrium processes does not depend on the concentration gradient.
a) True, False
b) True, True
c) False, True
d) False, False
a
5. Which of the following is untrue?
a) Membrane separation follows the law of conservation of energy
b) Membranes are used for separating components in food on a molecular basis
c) Membrane separation cannot be used in ultrafiltration of blood proteins
d) Reverse osmosis is an important application in membrane separation
c

6. Which of the following statements about unit operation performance are correct?
Statement 1: Performance of unit operation depends on material handling.
Statement 2: Optimization of time and movement of material from one unit operation to other is achieved by proper material handling techniques.
a) True, False
b) True, True
c) False, True
d) False, False
b
7. In an evaporator, the heat lost by heating water must be equal to the _____________
a) sum of heat gained by the product and heat lost from the evaporator to its surroundings
b) sum of heat lost from the evaporator to its surroundings in each step
c) heat gained by the product
d) overall heat lost from the evaporator
a
8. Preliminary unit operation in any food industry includes __________
a) cleaning, sorting
b) cleaning, drying
c) grading, drying
d) sorting, drying
a
9. What are the properties to be considered for designing a unit operation equipment?
a) Only Physical properties and mechanical properties
b) Only Chemical properties and mechanical properties
c) Only Physical properties and chemical properties
d) Physical, chemical, mechanical properties
d
10. Is mixing not considered as a unit operation?
a) True
b) False
b
