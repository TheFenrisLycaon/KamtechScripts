1. In a __________ pump, the fluid is drawn into pump and is then forced through outlet.
a) positive displacement
b) jet
c) airlift
d) propeller pumps
a
2. In _________pumps, the energy of the fluid is converted into velocity energy.
a) positive displacement
b) jet
c) airlift
d) propeller pumps
b
3. Which of the following pumps can be used as vacuum pumps?
a) Positive displacement
b) Jet
c) Airlift
d) Propeller pumps
b
4. Are these statements about the propeller pumps true?
Statement 1: Propellers are used to impart energy to the fluids.
Statement 2: They have low efficiency.
a) True, False
b) True, True
c) False, True
d) False, False
a
5. The _________pump converts rotational energy of the fluid to velocity and pressure.
a) centrifugal
b) jet
c) airlift
d) propeller pumps
a
6. Which of the following statements about airlift pumps are correct?
Statement 1: In airlift pumps, the air is introduced above a liquid in pressure level and that pressure is used to discharge the liquid.
Statement 2: Airlift pumps are used to convey powders in fluidized form.
a) True, False
b) True, True
c) False, True
d) False, False
b
7. What is significance of fan law?
a) It deals with pressure and velocity of the fan
b) It deals with pressure and power consumption of the fan
c) It deals with performance of the fan and power consumption of the fan
d) It deals with performance of the fan velocity of the fan
c
8. The efficiency of the pump is ratio of ____________
a) energy to velocity and pressure
b) energy to velocity
c) energy to pressure
d) velocity and pressure to energy
a
9. What are the factors to be considered for designing a centrifugal pump?
a) Capacity, Rotational speed only
b) Rotational speed only
c) Pump, Rotational speed only
d) Capacity, rotational speed and pump
d
10. Are pumps and fans used for conveying gases?
a) True
b) False
b
