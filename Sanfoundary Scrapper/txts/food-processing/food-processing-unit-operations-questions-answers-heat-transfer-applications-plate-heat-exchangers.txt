1. What is the purpose of the portholes on the plates of plate heat exchangers?
a) To have a even flow of the fluid throughout the plate
b) To drain the excess liquid from the plate
c) To maintain the pressure inside the heat exchanger
d) To stack the plates
a
2. The corrugation of V shape embossed on the plates is termed as _______________
a) reflex angle
b) V angle
c) Z angle
d) chevron angle
d
3. Which heat exchanger is most efficient?
a) Parallel flow
b) Cross flow
c) Counter flow
d) Tangent flow
c
