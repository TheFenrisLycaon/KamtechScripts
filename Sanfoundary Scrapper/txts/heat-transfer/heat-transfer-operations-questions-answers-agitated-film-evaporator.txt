1. Agitated film evaporators are best suited for_________
a) High corrosive fluids
b) Fouling fluids that usually scale
c) Highly viscous fluids
d) Highly volatile fluids
c
2. How do the Agitated film evaporator increase heat transfer coefficient?
a) It has a set of blades that keeps renewing the film
b) It has mixers that mix the fluid
c) It is a type of climbing film evaporator with agitators
d) It is a horizontal tube evaporator with agitated outer fluid
a
3. Which one of the following is a merit of agitated film evaporator?
a) Nucleate boiling
b) Falling film
c) Self cleaning equipment
d) High temperature management
c
4. The blades of the agitated film evaporator have a self cleaning effect on the evaporator and hence give us the freedom to use fouling fluids.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Which one of the following is not a property of agitated film evaporator?
a) Prevents the formation of hot spots due to agitation
b) Absence of static pressure on the boiling liquid
c) Staging or vapour recompression for energy recovery
d) Ability to work in continuous or batch mode
c
6. The residence time of agitated film evaporators is ______ than rising film evaporators.
a) More
b) Less
c) Very much higher
d) Negligible
b
7. Agitated film evaporator is sometimes more suitable because of which one of the following statement?
a) It has Low pressure operations
b) The temperature can be maintained as circulation is present
c) The flow velocity in the tubes and the liquid temperature can be controlled to suit the product requirements
d) Self cleaning because of presence of blades
d
8. Agitated film evaporator is also known as _______ film evaporator.
a) Wiped
b) Swiped
c) Flushed
d) Flash
a
9. Where does the agitation take place in an agitated film evaporator?
a) Before the liquid falls
b) At the film on the tube surface
c) At the upper end as fluid enters rest is turbulent
d) At the bottom-most point
b
10. Which one of the following is not an advantage of agitated film evaporator?
a) Elevated heat transfer coefficients due to agitation.
b) Very low operation cost
c) Ability of working at high vacuum (down to 0.001 mbar)
d) Very low residence times (to avoid product degradation)
b
11. Which one of the following is not a disadvantage of agitated film evaporator?
a) Cost of motors for agitators
b) Higher cost compared to standard evaporation equipment
c) Absence of static pressure on the boiling liquid
d) Staging or vapour recompression for energy recovery are not possible
c
i. Higher cost compared to standard evaporation equipment
ii. Staging or vapour recompression for energy recovery are not possible
Otherwise agitators are very useful and mostly preferred over others.
