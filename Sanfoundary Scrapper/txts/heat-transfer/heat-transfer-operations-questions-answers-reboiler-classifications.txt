1. Which one of the following is not a type of re-boiler?
a) Double pipe Reboilers
b) Thermosyphon reboilers
c) Kettle reboilers
d) Internal reboilers
a
2. Which one of the following is not a working property of re-boiler?
a) Generate a flux of vapour to feed to a distillation tower
b) Boil water at film boiling temperature
c) Flash boiling
d) Boil water at bubble formation region
d
3. The simplest re-boiler can be named as _______________ with the boiler just below the distillation column.
a) Flash reboilers
b) Internal re-boilers
c) Kettle reboilers
d) Horizontal thermosyphon reboiler
b
4. The kettle type reboiler is attached to the main body of the distillation column.
a) True
b) False
b
5. The reboiler which has its heating system mounted directly to the distillation column is ____________
a) Flash reboilers
b) Internal re-boilers
c) Kettle reboilers
d) Horizontal thermosyphon reboiler
b

6. Which one of the following reboiler is a thermosyphon reboiler?
a) Vertical
b) Slanted
c) Kettle
d) Internal
a
7. Which class of reboilers do not require pumps to pump the condensate back to the reboiler?
a) Thermosyphon reboiler
b) Flash reboiler
c) Kettle reboiler
d) Shell reboiler
a
8. Which one of the following reboiler uses a shell and tube heat exchanger?
a) Kettle reboiler
b) Vertical Thermosyphon reboiler
c) Flash reboiler
d) Internal reboiler
b
9. Which one of the following reboiler uses a 1-2shell and tube heat exchanger?
a) Kettle reboiler
b) Vertical Thermosyphon reboiler
c) Flash reboiler
d) Horizontal thermosyphon reboiler
d
10. The distillation procedure occurring by using a reboiler is known as ___________
a) Gas distillation
b) Normal distillation
c) Steam distillation
d) Oil distillation
c
11. In which one of the following reboilers, furnace is used to heat the condensate?
a) Kettle Reboilers
b) Fired reboilers
c) Vertical Thermosyphon reboiler
d) Horizontal thermosyphon reboiler
b
12. Which one of the following is best suited for operation of fouling as well as sensitive fluids?
a) Vertical Thermosyphon reboiler
b) Horizontal thermosyphon reboiler
c) Forced circulation reboilers
d) Kettle reboiler
c
