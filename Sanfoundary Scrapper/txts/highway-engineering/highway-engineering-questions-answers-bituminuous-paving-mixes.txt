1. Which layer of pavement should withstand stress?
a) Surface
b) Sub grade
c) Sub base
d) Base
a
2. The surface of bitumen should be __________
a) Smooth
b) Rough
c) Sufficient enough to resist friction
d) Very smooth
c
3. The DBM is used in __________
a) Local streets
b) Rural roads
c) Highway
d) Concrete bridges
c
4. The filler material should pass from __________
a) 0.075mm
b) 0.75mm
c) 7.5mm
d) 75mm
a
5. Rutting may be avoided by __________
a) Good compaction
b) Good aggregate

d) Good workmanship

6. Bleeding may be avoided by __________
a) Water voids
b) Air voids
c) Porosity
d) Water content

7. Which aggregate produce higher stability of mix?
a) Gravel
b) Sharp sand
c) Rounded sand
d) Fly ash

a) 25 mm
b) 50 mm

d) 30 mm

a) 18.5mm
b) 19.5mm
c) 20.5mm

a) 0.8 & 0.9
b) 0.95 & 0.97

a) VV+VB
