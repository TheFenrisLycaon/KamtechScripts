1. The loss with sodium sulphate cycles for aggregate used for bitumen is __________
a) Less than 12%
b) Less than 15%
c) Less than 16%
d) Less than 20%
a
2. How many kg of binder is applied for 10m2 area?
a) 68 kg
b) 71 kg
c) 82 kg
d) 93 kg
a
3. Built up spray grout consists of __________
a) One layer
b) Two layers
c) Three layers
d) Indefinite layers
b
4. The stripping of aggregate should be less than __________
a) 12
b) 25
c) 35
d) 45
b
5. The camber is checked at every interval of __________
a) 10 m
b) 20 m
c) 30 m
d) 40 m
a
6. What is the minimum thickness used in bituminous macadam?
a) 25 mm
b) 50 mm
c) 75 mm
d) 100 mm
c
7. The interface bond between the existing pavement and new pavement is provided by __________
a) Tack coat
b) Prime coat
c) Bitumen
d) Oil
a
8. The spreading test performed on bitumen can be checked on metal plates of dimensions is?
a) 200*200mm
b) 300*300mm
c) 400*400mm
d) 500*500mm
a
9. The water sensitivity of aggregates should retain __________
a) 75% of tensile strength
b) 80% of tensile strength
c) 95% of tensile strength
d) 100% of tensile strength
b
10. The polished stone value for aggregate of surface course should be more than __________
a) 15
b) 25
c) 35
d) 55
d
