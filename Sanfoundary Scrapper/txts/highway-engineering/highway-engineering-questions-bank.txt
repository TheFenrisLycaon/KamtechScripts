1. The measure not adopted in reconstruction of new highway is _____________
a) Providing effective drainage system
b) Designing as per traffic
c) Use of inferior quality material
d) Use of modern machinery
c
2. Common types of unsurfaced roads adopted in India are ___________
a) Gravel roads
b) WBM roads
c) Both gravel and WBM
d) Flexible pavements
c
3. The cross slope required for the correction of shoulder is?
a) 2%
b) 3%
c) 4%
d) 5%
d
4. The defects like pot holes, corrugation, rutting can be rectified by ___________
a) Trenching
b) Grading
c) Dragging
d) Chemical treatment
b
5. The re gravelled course is checked at a distance of every ___________
a) 10 m
b) 20 m
c) 50 m
d) 100 m
d
6. The dust palliative not used for reducing dust is ___________
a) Magnesium chloride
b) Calcium chloride
c) Molasses
d) Fly ash
d
7. The WBM mostly get damaged in ___________
a) Summer
b) Winter
c) Rainy season
d) Spring
c
8. The distress not caused in localised form is ___________
a) Pot hole
b) Isolated crack stress
c) Localised depression
d) Liquefaction
d
9. An existing flexible pavement that develops extensive cracks is called ___________
a) Ravelling
b) Alligator cracks
c) Shear
d) Pot hole
b
10. Shear failure are classified into how many types?
a) One
b) Two
c) Three
d) Four
b
