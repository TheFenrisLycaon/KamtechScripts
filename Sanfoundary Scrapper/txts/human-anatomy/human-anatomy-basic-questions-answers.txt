1. Which of the following bone is called collar bone?
a) Acetabulum
b) Pelvic
c) Scapula
d) Clavicle
d
2. Glenoid cavity is found in _________
a) Pelvic girdle
b) Pectoral girdle
c) Sternum
d) Humerous
b
3. Name of the shoulder bone is _________
a) Acetabulum
b) Pelvic
c) Scapula
d) Clavicle
c
4. Colle’s fracture is associated with ___________
a) Femur
b) Ulna
c) Radius
d) Humerus
c
5. The total number of bones found in right upper limb is ____________
a) 25
b) 26
c) 30
d) 60
c
6. An acromian process is characteristically found in ____________
a) Pelvic girdle of mammals
b) Skull of frog
c) Pectoral girdle of mammals
d) Sperm of mammals
c
7. Which cup lies at the junction of 3 bones of pelvic girdle?
a) Ilium
b) Ischium
c) Pubis
d) Acetabulum
d
8. Ilium, ischium and pubis are three bones of a pelvic girdle of man.
a) True
b) False
a
9. Which of the following is known as a knee cap?
a) Acetabulum
b) Scapula
c) Patella
d) Clavicle
c
