1. The blood corpuscles are of _________ kinds.
a) 5
b) 4
c) 2
d) 3
d
2. Blood is stained with _________ stain.
a) Methylene blue
b) Safranin
c) Leishman stain
d) Carbol fuchsine
c
3. Process of formation of blood corpuscles is called _____________
a) Haemolysis
b) Haemozoin
c) Haemopoesis
d) Haemoter
c
4. Graveyard of RBC is ________
a) Spleen
b) Liver
c) Kidney
d) Thymus
a
5. Which leucocytes release heparin and histamine in blood?
a) Neutrophil
b) Basophil
c) Eosinophil
d) Monocytes
b
6. Which blood cells secrets antibody?
a) Eosinophils
b) Monocytes
c) Lymphocytes
d) Neutrophils
c
7. Vitamin essential for blood clotting is _____________
a) Vitamin K
b) Vitamin A
c) Vitamin B
d) Vitamin C
a
8. Hemoglobin is a ___________
a) Reproductive pigment
b) Respiratory pigment
c) Carbohydrate
d) Fat
b
9. Absence of which clotting factor leads to Hemophilia-A?
a) Factor VII
b) Factor VIII
c) Factor IX
d) Factor X
b
10. What prevents the clotting of blood inside blood vessels?
a) Heparin
b) Serotonin
c) Fibrinogen
d) Fibrin
a
11. Red cell count is carried out by _________
a) Electrogram
b) Sphygmomanometer
c) Haemoglobinometer
d) Haemocytometer
d
12. Blood is five times more viscous than distilled water.
a) True
b) False
a
