1. The outside layer of the oocyte is the ______________
a) Zona pellucida
b) Theca interna

2. The mature follicle is also called as _____________
a) Secondary follicle

c) Granulosa cell

b) Blastula
c) Graafian follicle
d) Ovary
c

a) Female genital tract
b) Vas deferentia
c) Vagina
d) Penis
