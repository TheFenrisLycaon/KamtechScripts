1. What occurs when egg and sperm fuse?
a) Differentiation
b) Development
c) Fertilization
d) Cleavage
c
2. Which among the following is the cell division without growth?
a) Cleavage
b) Conjugation
c) Fertilization
d) Development
a
3. ___________ is a term for milk production.
a) Lactation
b) Parturition
c) Menstruation
d) Ovulation
a
4. Hormones that cause uterus to contact are ____________
a) FSH LH
b) Prolactin and progesterone
c) Estrogen and progesterone
d) Oxytocin and prostaglandins
d
5. Which hormone is the basis for pregnancy test?
a) HCG
b) Prolactin
c) Estrogen
d) Prostaglandins
a
6. In the 3rd week embryo begins the implantation.
a) True
b) False
b
7. The lining of uterus to which the embryo implants is called as ______________
a) Coccyx
b) Trohoblast
c) Yolk plug
d) Endometrium
d
8. The initial step during fertilization is _________________
a) Penetration of sperm into egg
b) Fertilizin and antifertilizin reaction
c) Formation of fertilization membrane
d) Formation of fertilization cone
b
9. Fertilization of sperm and ovum takes place in _____________
a) Ampulla of oviduct
b) Isthmus of oviduct
c) Fimbriae of oviduct
d) Uterus
a
10. The extra embryonic structure that provides nutrition to the embryo is ______________
a) Chorion
b) Placenta
c) Amnion
d) Umblicus
b
11. Beginning of menstruation is known as _______________
a) Menopause
b) Ovulation
c) Oogenesis
d) Menarche
d
12. The main function if fimbriae of oviduct are _____________
a) Help in collection of ovum after ovulation
b) Make necessary changes in endometrium for implantation
c) Release ovum from Graafian follicle
d) Help in devilment of embryo
a
13. The process of delivery of fetus is known as _____________
a) Lactation
b) Implantation
c) Parturition
d) Fertilization
c
14. In the absence of acrosome the sperm cannot ________________
a) Penetrate into egg
b) Get energy
c) Get food
d) Swim
a
