1. Which among the following is the longest?
a) Stomach
b) Esophagus
c) Small intestine
d) Large intestine
c
2. Small intestine is divided into __________ parts.
a) 1
b) 2
c) 4
d) 3
d
3. The opening of Ileum is guarded by ___________
a) Ileo caecal valve
b) Mitral valve
c) Sphincter
d) Villi
a
4. Secretin and cholecystokinin are digestive hormones. These are secreted by __________
a) Esophagus
b) Stomach
c) Ileum
d) Duodenum
d
5. “Crypts of Lieberkuhn” are found in ___________
a) Gall bladder
b) Intestine
c) Liver
d) Kidney
b

a) Colon
b) Intestine
c) Liver
d) Kidney

a) Duodenum
b) Vermiform appendix
c) Peyer’s patch
d) Plicae circulares

a) Colon
b) Caecum
c) Rectum
d) Kidney
