1. Grave’s disease is due to __________
a) Hypoactivity of Islets of Langerhans
b) Hyperactivity of adrenal cortex
c) Hyperactivity of thyroid gland

2. Hypothyroidism in adult causes _________

b) Cretinism
c) Diabetes
