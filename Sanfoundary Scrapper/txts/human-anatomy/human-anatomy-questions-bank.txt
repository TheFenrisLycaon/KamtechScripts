1. The zygote undergoes cleavage forming a solid ball of cells of the uniform size called _________
a) Morula
b) Blastula
c) Gastrula
d) Neurula
a
2. The third layer to form in gastrulation is ____________
a) Archenteron
b) Ectoderm
c) Endoderm
d) Mesoderm
d
3. Development of all organs can be traced in single germ layer.
a) True
b) False
a
4. The trophoblast gives rise to ___________
a) Chorion
b) Neurula
c) Allantois
d) Placenta
a
5. Gastrulation occurs during the 2nd week of embryonic development.
a) True
b) False
a
6. Cleavage begins at ____________
a) Vagina
b) Ovary
c) Uterus
d) Oviduct
d
7. The cavity inside the blastula is called as _____________
a) Blastocoel
b) Blastomere
c) Gastrula
d) Animal pore
a
8. The vertebrate embryo develops into hollow ball called as _______________
a) Fistula
b) Blastula
c) Gastrula
d) Morula
b
9. The embryo at 16 celled stage is known as ___________
a) Morula
b) Gastrula
c) Blastula
d) Blastomere
a
10. Blastopore is the opening of _____________
a) Blastomere
b) Blastula
c) Gastrula
d) Archenteron
d
11. At the time of implantation the embryo is called as ________
a) Blastocyst
b) Zygote
c) Fetus
d) Morula
a
