1. Which part of pancreas produce and secrete insulin?
a) Glomerulus
b) Bowman’s capsule
c) Islets of Langerhans
d) Loop of Henle
c
2. Alpha cells are found in _________ of the islet while beta cells are usually found in the __________ of the islet.
a) Periphery, center
b) Center, periphery
c) Equally in both regions, periphery
d) Periphery, Equally in both regions
a
3. Secretion of pancreatic juice is stimulated by ___________
a) Gastrin
b) Secretin
c) Enterokinase
d) Enterogastron
b
4. Which of the following is the common passage for bile and pancreatic juice?
a) Duct of oddi
b) Ampulla
c) Stomach
d) Duct of Wirsung
b
5. Principal fat digesting enzyme is in pancreatic juice.
a) True
b) False
a
6. The pancreatic juice which aids digestion is secreted into?
a) Liver
b) Duodenum
c) Stomach
d) Ileum
b
7. A person can survive without pancreas.
a) True
b) False
a
8. Which cells produce insulin?
a) Alpha cells
b) Beta cells
c) Delta cells
d) F cells
a
