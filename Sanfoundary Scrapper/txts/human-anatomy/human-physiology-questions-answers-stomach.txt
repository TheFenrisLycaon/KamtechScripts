1. Which of the following cells produces HCL?
a) Beta cells
b) Oxyntic cells
c) Chief cells
d) Alpha cells
b
2. The gastric juice contains _________
a) Trypsin, pepsin, lipase
b) Pepsin, lipase, rennin
c) Pepsin, amylase, trypsin
d) Trypsin, pepsin, rennin
b
3. Digestion of protein is completed in _________
a) Stomach
b) Ileum
c) Duodenum
d) Duodenum and Ileum
c
4. Enzyme rennin is secreted by _________
a) Stomach
b) Liver
c) Kidney
d) Pancreas
a
5. Jaundice is the disorder of ______________
a) Excretory system
b) Digestive system
c) Circulatory system
d) Skin and eyes
b
6. Which of the following converts pepsinogen to the active form of pepsin in the stomach?
a) Mucus
b) Gastrin
c) HCL
d) Chef cells
c
7. Secretion of Gastric juice is stimulated by?
a) Gastrin
b) Cholecystokinin
c) Enterogastrin
d) Pepsin
a
8. Obesity is defined as a body weight of more than 20% above the ideal weight.
a)  True
b) False
a
