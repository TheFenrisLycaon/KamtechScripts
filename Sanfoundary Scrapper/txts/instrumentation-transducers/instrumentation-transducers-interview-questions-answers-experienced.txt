1. Process of physical deformation on application of electric field is described by ___________
a) Electromagnetic property
b) Electro mechanical property
c) Magnetostriction property
d) Electrostriction property
d
2. Who invented the piezoelectric effect?
a) Mary Elizabeth Barber
b) Christian Doppler
c) Marie curie and Pierre curie
d) Pierre curie and Jacques curie
d
3. Which of the following represents piezoelectric materials?
a) ADP
b) Quartz
c) Bernilite
d) All of the mentioned
d
4. Property of exhibiting electric polarization when exposed to intense electric field is known as ___________
a) Electromagnetic effect
b) Ferromagnetic material
c) Ferroelectric materials
d) Piezoelectric materials
c
5. Which of the following quantities cannot be measured using piezoelectric transducers?
a) Pressure
b) Strain
c) Acceleration
d) None of the mentioned
d
6. Which of the following can be used conveniently at 1000c temperature?
a) Rochelle salt
b) ADP
c) Both ADP and Rochelle salt
d) None of the mentioned
b
7. Which of the following type transducers are used in IC engines?
a) Monomorph
b) Dimorph
c) Multimorph
d) All of the mentioned
c
8. __________ are also known as piezopile.
a) Monomorph
b) Dimorph
c) Multimorph
d) None of the mentioned
c
9. Piezoelectric accelerometer requires damping system.
a) True
b) False
b
10. In piezoelectric strain transducer voltage developed is _______________ to strain applied.
a) Directly proportional
b) Inversely proportional
c) Equal
d) Independent
a
