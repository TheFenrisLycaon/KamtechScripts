1. In a time interval measuring device, two pulses are used ______________
a) To produce triangular wave
b) To start and stop gate circuit
c) To produce sinusoidal signal
d) None of the mentioned
b
2. Start and stop pulses are produced by ______________
a) Gate circuit
b) Time discriminators
c) Oscillators
d) None of the mentioned
b
3. TDC devices are used for ________________
a) Indicating frequency of signal
b) Indicating time delay in analog format
c) Indicating time delay in digital format
d) Indicating voltage level
c
4. Measuring range is not an important parameter in TIM.
a) True
b) False
b
5. When interpolation techniques are used?
a) For high range of measurement is required
b) For high resolution of measurement
c) For high range of measurement and high resolution of the measurement is required
d) Not the mentioned
c
6. What is the use of PWM technology?
a) To amplify output voltage
b) To reduce output voltage
c) For controlling supply voltage
d) For measuring voltage
c
7. What do you mean by duty cycle?
a) Ratio of on time and total time period
b) Ratio of off time and total time period
c) On time
d) Off time
a
8. Time require by the signal to reach 63% of amplitude is known as _____________
a) Duty cycle
b) Time delay
c) Time constant
d) On time
c
9. What is the use of a gate circuit in TIM?
a) To produce sinusoidal signal
b) To produce pulse signal
c) To on and off input signal
d) None of the mentioned
c
10. TDC and TIM are used for the same application.
a) True
b) False
a
