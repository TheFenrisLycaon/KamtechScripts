1. All bodies behave as source of _______________
a) Electromagnetic radiation
b) IR radiation
c) Ultra violet radiation
d) None of the mentioned
a
2. Which of the following represent a spectrum of frequency emission?
a) Absorption spectra
b) Emission spectra
c) EM spectra
d) None of the mentioned
b
3. Spectrography doesn’t compute the energy of frequency spectrum.
a) True
b) False
b
4. Which of the following source is used for exciting bodies electrically?
a) DC arc
b) AC arc
c) Spark
d) All of the mentioned
d

a) Graphite
b) Carbon

b) Band spectra
c) Line spectra
d) All of the mentioned

b) False

b) Grating
