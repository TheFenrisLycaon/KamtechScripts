1. Which of the following can be measured using tachometers?
a) Angular speed
b) Linear speed
c) Acceleration
d) Vibration
a
2. Two phase induction motor can be used as AC tachometers.
a) True
b) False
a
3. Electrodynamic vibration transducers are based on _____________
a) Magnetostriction
b) Electromagnetic induction
c) Self inductance
d) None of the mentioned
b

a) Low impedance voltmeter
b) High impedance voltmeter
c) Any voltmeter
d) None of the mentioned
b

a) It causes obstruction to flow
b) It doesn’t cause obstruction t flow
c) It cannot be used in slurries

b

b) Density

b) BV
