1. A decoder converts n inputs to __________ outputs.
a) n
b) n2
c) 2n
d) nn
c
2. Which of the following are building blocks of encoders?
a) NOT gate
b) OR gate
c) AND gate
d) NAND gate
b
3. Which of the following can be represented for decoder?
a) Sequential circuit
b) Combinational circuit
c) Logical circuit
d) None of the mentioned
b
4. BCD to seven segment conversion is a ________________
a) Decoding process
b) Encoding process
c) Comparing process
d) None of the mentioned
a
5. Which of the following is a decoder IC?
a) 7890
b) 8870
c) 4047
d) 4041
b
6. DTMF stands for ________________
a) Dual Tone Magnetic Frequency
b) Double Tone Magnetic Frequency
c) Dual Tone Multiple Frequency
d) Dual Tone Mechanical Frequency
c
7. Invalid BCD can be made to valid BCD by adding with _______________
a) 0101
b) 0110
c) 0111
d) 1001
b
8. Decoder is constructed from ________________
a) Inverters
b) AND gates
c) Inverters and AND gates
d) None of the mentioned
c
9. Which of the following represents a number of output lines for a decoder with 4 input lines?
a) 15
b) 16
c) 17
d) 18
b
10. Decoders and Encoders are doing reverse operation.
a) True
b) False
a
