1. Semiconductor used in sensors will be _____________
a) Pure form
b) Doped form
c) Pure or doped form
d) None of the mentioned
c
2. A semiconductor can act as ___________________
a) Insulator
b) Semi conductor
c) Pure conductor
d) All of the mentioned
d
3. Which of the following represent permalloy?
a) Nickel iron alloy
b) Nickel platinum alloy
c) Nickel gold alloy
d) Nickel bronze alloy
a
4. Permalloy are most suitable for _______________
a) Pressure measurement
b) Temperature measurement
c) Voltage measurement
d) All of the mentioned
b
5. Silicon and germanium can act as piezo resistive materials.
a) True
b) False
a
6. Which of the following act as magneto-resistive material?
a) Bismuth
b) Antimonide
c) Both bismuth and antimonide
d) None of the mentioned
c
7. In piezo junction diode, energy domain will be ______________
a) Electrical
b) Mechanical
c) Radiation
d) Thermal
b

a) Poly Vinyledene Fluoride
b) Poly Vinyle Duro Fluoride
c) Penta Valent Di Fluoride

b) 8 VK-1
