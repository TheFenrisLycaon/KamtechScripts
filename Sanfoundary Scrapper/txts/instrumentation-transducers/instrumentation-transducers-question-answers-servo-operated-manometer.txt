1. Which of the following materials can be used in high pressure manometer tube?
a) Glass
b) Stainless steel
c) Transparent plastic tube
d) None of the mentioned
b
2. Band width of servo operated manometers are limited to ___________________
a) 5 Hz
b) 5 KHz
c) 5 MHz
d) 5 GHz
a
3. Manometers are generally used to measure _______________
a) Pressure
b) Voltage
c) Temperature
d) Humidity
a
4. Atmospheric pressure can be expressed in _______________
a) N
b) N/m
c) N/m2
d) None of the mentioned
c
5. By conversion, 1 bar= _________ N/m2.
a) 104
b) 105
c) 106
d) 107
b
6. Which of the following represents pressure exerted by a column of mercury of 1 mm?
a) Micron
b) Torr
c) Microbar
d) Bar
b
7. Which of the following is the lowest unit for pressure?
a) Bar
b) Micron
c) Torr
d) Microbar
b
8. Water is non-manometric liquid.
a) True
b) False
b
9. Which of the following has a low evaporation rate than water?
a) Mercury
b) Transformer oil
c) Tetrabromoethane
d) All of the mentioned
d
10. As specific gravity increases, the evaporation rate of manometric liquid decreases.
a) True
b) False
b
