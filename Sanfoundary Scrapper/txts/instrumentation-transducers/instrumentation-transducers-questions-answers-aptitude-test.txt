1. Ion-isoconcentration technique is used for determination of __________________
a) Humidity
b) Water in organic solvent
c) pH of solution
d) None of the mentioned
b
2. Which of the following is immiscible in water?
a) Perchloro ethelyne
b) Ethanol
c) Acetone
d) All of the mentioned
a
3. PICT stands for ______________________
a) Proton iso-concentration technique
b) Perchloro ion concentration technique
c) Proton iso control technique
d) Proton inhibition cell technique
a
4. PICT has high sensitivity than FICT.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Which of the following represents RH value of moisture contained sample?
a) Water activity
b) Moisture content
c) Ion concentration
d) Acidity
a
6. Which of the following IR radiation is used in measuring relative humidity?
a) Wavelength of less than 1um
b) Wavelength less than 3um
c) Wavelength greater than 10um
d) Wavelength of any value
b
7. Which of the following is not used for measuring moisture?
a) IR absorption method
b) Microwave loss method
c) Both IR absorption and microwave loss method
d) None of the mentioned
d
8. For moisture measurement, microwave of ____________ wavelength is used.
a) Less than 3um
b) Less than 3nm
c) Less than 3mm
d) less than 3cm
d
9. FICT stands for ______________
a) Forced iso concentration technique
b) Flouro iso concentration technique
c) Flouro ion concentration technique
d) Flouro ion carry transfer
b
10. Which of the following is correct for water activity?
a) Ratio of relative equilibrium moisture and 10
b) Ratio of relative equilibrium moisture and 100
c) Ratio of relative equilibrium moisture and 1000
d) Relative equilibrium moisture
b
