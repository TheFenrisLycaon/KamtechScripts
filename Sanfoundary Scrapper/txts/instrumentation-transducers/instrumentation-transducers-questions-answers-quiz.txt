1. Wheatstone bridge is used to measure ________________
a) Resistance
b) Inductance
c) Capacitance
d) All of the mentioned
d
2. Which of the following represents the arrangement of Wheatstone bridge?
a) Potentiometer
b) Weinbridge
c) Schering bridge
d) None of the mentioned
b
3. Which of the following is not an AC bridge?
a) Kelvin bridge
b) Maxwell bridge
c) Hay bridge
d) Schering bridge
a
4. Anderson bridge is an AC bridge.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Which of the following represents DC Bridge?
a) Used to measure impedance
b) AC supply for excitation
c) Wein bridge
d) None of the mentioned
d
