6. Convert the hydropower potential of 84 GW at 60% load factor into Trillion watts hour.
a) 441 TWh
b) 735 TWh
c) 553 TWh
d) 345 TWh
a
84 GW is equivalent to = 84 x 365 x 24 = 735,840 GW hour
At 60 % load factor = 735,840 x 0.6 = 441,504 GW hour = 441 TWh or 441 b KWh.
