1. Which of the following phylum is known for its asymmetrical body shape?
a) Mollusca
b) Arthropoda
c) Annelida
d) Porifera
d
2. Name the phylum which has a single opening for taking food and throwing out faeces?
a) Coelenterate
b) Porifera
c) Annelida
d) Aschelminthes
a
3. Name the organism which has flame-cell in its excretory system?
a) Corals
b) Ctenophora
c) Roundworm
d) Flatworm
d
4. Which of the following is causative agent of Trichionosis?
a) Trichinella spiralis
b) Oxytricha trifallax
c) Morus nigra
d) Agrodiaetus shahrami
a
5. Intestinal fluke disease is caused by phylum__________
a) Ctenophora
b) Platyhelminthes
c) Annelida
d) Aschelminthes
b
6. Which organism has segmental nephridia for excretion and osmoregulation?
a) Platyhelminthes
b) Aschelminthes
c) Annelida
d) Arthropoda
c
7. In arthropods, Ecdysis or moulting is a process of casting off the skin.
a) True
b) False
a
8. Which of the following phylum has a water vascular system?
a) Plantae
b) Mollusca
c) Echinodermata
d) Hemichordata
c
9. Name the phylum which is characterized by the presence of notochord.
a) Chordata
b) Hemichordata
c) Echinodermata
d) Mollusca
a
10. Name the first vertebrate which possessed amniotic egg?
a) Amphibia
b) Aves
c) Reptilia
d) Mammalia
a
11. Which of the following is known as scavengers in the marine ecosystem?
a) Lamprey
b) Hagfish
c) Scoliodon
d) Pristis
b
