1. The ability of an organism to resist infections by the pathogens is called___________
a) Infection
b) Hypersensitivity
c) Immunity
d) Allergy
c
2. Innate immunity present since birth and it has no memory.
a) True
b) False
a

a) Interferons

a) CRP
b) Toll-like receptor
