1. The process by which bacterial cell uptake DNA is called___________
a) Gene expression
b) Transformation
c) Transcription
d) Translation
d
2. Electroporation method uses an electric pulse to facilitate uptake of DNA.
a) True
b) False
a
3. Which of the following chemical is used in chemical transformation method?
a) Cu
b) FeSO4
c) SO2
d) CaCl2
d
4. Which of the following method creates pores in the membrane?
a) Gene gun
b) Chemical transformation
c) Electroporation
d) Agrobacterium
c
5. Which of the following particles is used in the gene gun method?
a) Iron particles
b) Aluminum particles
c) Silver particles
d) Gold particles
d
6. Which of the following shows the location of the inserted genes?
a) Palindromic sequences
b) Resistance gene
c) Restriction site
d) Marker gene
d
7. Which of the following is NOT true for agrobacterium?
a) Horizontal gene transfer
b) Gram positive bacteria
c) Causes tumor in plants
d) Transfer DNA in plants
b
8. Name the disease caused by Agrobacterium in plants?
a) Crown gall disease
b) Black knot
c) Downy Mildew
d) Early Blight
a
