1. What is the basic functional and structural unit of organisms?
a) Nucleus
b) DNA
c) Cell
d) Gene
c
2. Viruses are cellular organisms.
a) True
b) False
b
3. Name the Scientists who first discovered the cell in the piece of cork?
a) Louis Pasteur
b) Anton van Leeuwenhoek
c) Robert Hooke
d) Rudolf Virchow
c
4. Which of the following is not the part of modern cell theory?
a) All living things are made up of one or more cells
b) The cell is a functional and structural unit of life
c) Energy flow takes place within the cell
d) All cells do not have the same chemical composition
d
5. What is the permeability of the plasma membrane?
a) Selectively permeable
b) Impermeable
c) Single phase flow
d) Highly permeable
a
6. Which of the following is described by the fluid mosaic model?
a) Nucleus
b) Plasma membrane
c) Endoplasmic reticulum
d) Ribosome
b
7. Mark the component which is not the part of lipid bilayer?
a) Glycerol or Sphingosine
b) Fatty acids
c) Tryptophan and methionine
d) Phosphate
c
8. What is the name of the hollow sphere formed by lipid bilayer?
a) Cholesterol
b) Lipid raft
c) Micelle
d) Liposome
d
9. Which of the following is ABC transport protein that transport lipid in opposite direction?
a) ATPase
b) Scramblase
c) Floppases
d) Flippase
c
10. Out of the following, which is not an ATP dependent transporter of lipid?
a) V-type ATPase
b) Scramblase
c) Flippase
d) Floppases
b
11. Name the technique which is used to visualize the lateral movement of lipids?
a) FRAP
b) Microscopy
c) Electrophoresis
d) Spectrometry
a
