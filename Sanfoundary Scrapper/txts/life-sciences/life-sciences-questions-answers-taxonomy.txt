1. Which one of the following is NOT covered under Taxonomy?
a) Alpha taxonomy
b) Beta taxonomy
c) Delta taxonomy
d) Gamma taxonomy
c
2. Organizing taxonomic information in logical classification is called_________
a) Systematics
b) Phenetic
c) Phylogenetic
d) Dendogram
a
3. Name the philosopher who first classified organisms?
a) Whittekar
b) Carl woese
c) Linnaeus
d) Aristotle
d
4. Name the organization which provides rules for naming animals.
a) ICZN
b) ICN
c) ICBN
d) IBM
a
5. Who is considered as the father of taxonomy?
a) Aristotle
b) Linnaeus
c) Earnst Haeckel
d) Whittaker
b
6. What is tautonym?
a) These are the repeated sequences
b) It is a name of fish
c) Identical name of genus and species
d) It is a name of the genus
c

1) Class
2) Kingdom
3) Phylum
4) Order
5) Genus
6) Family

b) 7, 1, 3, 4, 5, 6, 1

8. What is the term given to a duplicate specimen of original type?
a) Lectotype
b) Holotype
c) Isotype
d) Neotype
c
9. Phenetic classification does not distinguish between homologous and analogous characters.
a) True
b) False
a

b) Wings of birds and bats
