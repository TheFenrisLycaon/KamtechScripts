1. Most abundant and widely distributed tissues of the body is___________
a) Organ system
b) Muscular tissue
c) Nervous tissue
d) Connective tissue
d
2. All connective tissues are highly vascular nature.
a) True
b) False
b
3. Which of the following does not come under the category of loose connective tissue?
a) Elastic connective tissue
b) Adipose tissue
c) Areolar tissue
d) Reticular connective tissue
a
4. Name the tissue, which stores triglycerides (fats)?
a) Regular connective tissue
b) Areolar tissue
c) Adipose tissue
d) Epithelial tissue
c
5. Which of the following is NOT the function of areolar connective tissues?
a) Strength
b) Elasticity
c) Support
d) Forms stroma of organs
d
6. Mark the one, which is not the location of the hyaline cartilage?
a) End of long bones
b) Menisci of the knee
c) Anterior end of ribs
d) Nose
b
7. Among the following which one is not the location of smooth muscle cells?
a) Iris of the eye
b) Airways to the lungs
c) Heart wall
d) Urinary bladder
c
8. Mark the one, which is NOT the location of endocrine glands?
a) Pituitary gland
b) Salivary gland
c) Adrenal glands
d) Thyroid
b
