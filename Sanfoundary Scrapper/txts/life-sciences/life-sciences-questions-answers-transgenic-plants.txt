1. The process of expression of foreign genes in a plant is called___________
a) Gene expression
b) Transgenesis
c) Genetic transformation
d) Cell hybridization
c
2. In transgenesis, only cloned genes are introduced into the donor.
a) True
b) False
a
3. Name the strategy where two-plasmid system is used for the introduction of the gene?
a) Binary vector system
b) Co-integration vector strategy
c) Agrobacterium
d) Selectable marker strategy
a
4. Which of the following is considered as a visual marker?
a) Antibiotic marker
b) Resistance marker
c) Selectable marker
d) Screenable marker
d
5. Name the marker gene of streptomycin?
a) HPT
b) SPT
c) PAT
d) BXN
b
6. Name the first transgenic virus resistant plant?
a) Rice
b) Cotton
c) Tobacco
d) Tomato
c
7. Which of the following is supplemented with vitamin A in order to improve its nutritional quality?
a) Cotton
b) Potato
c) Tomato
d) Rice
d
8. Name the enzyme which has silenced to delay the ripening process?
a) Polygalacturonase
b) Glyphosate
c) ADA
d) P53
a
9. Name the technique which is used to enhance the life of a tomato.
a) Antisense technology
b) In vitro gene transfer
c) Ex vivo gene transfer
d) Molecular farming
a
