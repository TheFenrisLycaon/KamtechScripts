1. Light dependent changes in plant’s growth and development apart from photosynthesis is known as___________
a) Phytochrome
b) Dark reaction
c) Light reaction
d) Photomorphogenesis
d
2. Name the red light sensitive system?
a) Phytochrome
b) Cryptochrome
c) Phototropin

b) Bilirubin
