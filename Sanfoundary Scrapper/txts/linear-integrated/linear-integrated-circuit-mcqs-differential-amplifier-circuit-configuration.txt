11. Obtain the collector voltage, for collector resistor (RC) =5.6kΩ, IE=1.664mA and VCC=10v for single input unbalanced output differential amplifier
a) 0.987v
b) 0.682v
c) 0.555v
d) None of the mentioned
b
VC= VCC – RC×IC
⇒ VC= 10v – 5.6kΩ×1.664mA (∵ IC ≅ IE )
⇒ VC= 0.682v.
