1. According to specific use, drill can be classified as______
a) center drill
b) step drill
c) both center drill and step drill
d) none of the mentioned
c
2. Subland drill is type of drill according to______
a) l/d ratio
b) shank type
c) specific use
d) none of the mentioned
c

b) subland drill

b) tailstock
