b) Water vapour

a) True
b) False
b

a) Humidification
b) Evaporation
c) Heating of gas
d) None of the mentioned
b
5. The removal of acetone from acetone-benzene mixture is drying
a) True
b) False
b

b) Unbound moisture

a) 0.2
b) 0.42
c) 0.5
d) None of the mentioned

a) True
b) False
b

b) Batch
c) Semi batch

a) Bound moisture
b) Unbound moisture
c) Equilibrium moisture
d) Free moisture
