1. __________ is the single stage device in extractor types.

b) Sieve trays
c) Agitator vessel

a) True
b) False
a
3. The substance to get mixed enters into the mixer through.
a) Orifice
b) Nozzle
c) Orifice or nozzle
d) None of the mentioned
c

a) Small
b) Large
c) Very high
d) None of the mentioned
a

b) False

b) Heat transfer

b) Pulse column
