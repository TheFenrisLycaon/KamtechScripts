1. What is the impulse response of a system whose transfer function is that of ramp function delayed by 7 units in time?
a) r(t-7)
b) r(t+7)
c) r(t-3.5)
d) Cannot be determined
a

b) u(t)-u(t-3)

d) u(t)-u(t+4)
