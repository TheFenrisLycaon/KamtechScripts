1. Reinforcement of SiC fibers increases fracture toughness of glass ceramic to about ____________
a) 2 times
b) 3 times
c) 4 times
d) 5 times
a
2. Reinforcement of fibers of SiC improve flexural strength of glass ceramic to almost ____________
a) 2 times
b) 3 times
c) 4 times
d) 6 times
c
3. What is an effect of an increase in diameter of fiber on strength?
a) Increases linearly
b) Decreases linearly
c) Remains constant
d) Increases non-linearly
b
4. What is the concentration of particles by volume in dispersion strengthened composites?
a) Less than 1%
b) 1 – 15%
c) 15-20%
d) 25-30%
b
5. What is the size range in dispersoids in dispersion strengthened composites?
a) Less than 1 nm
b) 1 – 10 nm
c) 0.01 – 0.1 µm
d) 0.1 – 1 µm
c

b) 1 – 50 µm

b) Discontinuous
