1. Which of the following is an external interrupt?
a) INT0(active low)
b) INT2(active low)
c) Timer0 interrupt
d) Timer1 interrupt

a) IE0 and IE1
b) IE0 and IF1
c) IF0 and IE1
d) IF0 and IF1

a) level-sensitive
b) edge-sensitive
c) in serial port
d) in parallel port

4. If the external interrupt sources control the flags IE0 and IE1, then the interrupt programmed is
a) level-sensitive
b) edge-sensitive
c) in serial port
d) in parallel port

a) timer mode
b) counter mode
