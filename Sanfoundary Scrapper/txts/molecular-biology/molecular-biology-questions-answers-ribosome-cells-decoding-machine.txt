1. How many polypeptides are required for the structural assembly of the ribosome?
a) 10
b) Less than 10
c) 50
d) More than 50
d
2. Which of the following is the slowest process among the following?
a) Replication
b) Translation
c) Splicing
d) Transcription
b
3. The ribosome is designed in such a way so as to keep up with the speed of the RNA polymerase.
a) True
b) False
a
4. Transcription and translation occur in the same compartment in the cases of both prokaryotes and eukaryotes.
a) True
b) False
b
5. Transcriptional coupling ensures a fast rate f translation.
a) True
b) False

6. Ribosome has two subunits with 4 rRNA molecules. Which of the four rRNAs is found in the decoding center of the ribosome?
a) 5S
b) 23S
c) 28S
d) 16S
d
7. The large ribosomal subunit in prokaryotes has the sedimentation velocity of ___________
a) 30S
b) 40S

b) mass

i. Binding of the large and small subunit
