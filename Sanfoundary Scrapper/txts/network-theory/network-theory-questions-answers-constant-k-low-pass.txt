1. A network either T or π, is said to be of the constant-k type if Z1 and Z2 of the network satisfies the relation?
a) Z1Z2 = k
b) Z1Z2 = k2
c) Z1Z2 = k3
d) Z1Z2 = k4
b

a) jωL
b) 2 jωL
