1. If a resistor Rx is connected between nodes X and Y, Ry between X and Y, Rz between Y and Z to form a delta connection, then after transformation to star, the resistor at node X is?
a) RxRy/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
b) RxRz/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
c) RzRy/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
d) (Rx+Ry)/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
a
2. If a resistor Rx is connected between nodes X and Y, Ry between X and Y, Rz between Y and Z to form a delta connection, then after transformation to star, the resistance at node Y is?
a) RzRy/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
b) RzRx/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
c) RxRy/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
d) (Rz+Ry)/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
b
3. If a resistor Rx is connected between nodes X and Y, Ry between X and Y, Rz between Y and Z to form a delta connection, then after transformation to star, the resistance at node Z is?
a) RyRx/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
b) RyRx/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
c) RzRy/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
d) (Rz+Rx)/(Rx+Ry+Rz)
c
4. If the resistors of star connected system are R1, R2, R3 then the resistance between 1 and 2 in delta connected system will be?
