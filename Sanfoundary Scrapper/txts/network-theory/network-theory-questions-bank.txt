1. Two ports containing no sources in their branches are called?
a) active ports
b) passive ports
c) one port
d) three port
b
2. Two ports containing sources in their branches are called?
a) three port
b) one port
c) passive ports
d) active ports
d

b) I1 and I2
c) V1 and I2
