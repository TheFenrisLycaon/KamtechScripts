3. What are the levels in QoS?
a) Best effort service
b) Soft QoS
c) Hard QoS
d) All of the mentioned
d

a) Best effort service
b) Soft QoS
c) Worst effort service
d) Hard QoS
b
5. The quality of service requirements are guaranteed in ___________
a) Best effort service
b) Soft QoS
c) Worst effort service
d) Hard QoS
d

a) Throughput
b) Jitter
c) Delay
d) All of the mentioned
d

a) mean the same thing
