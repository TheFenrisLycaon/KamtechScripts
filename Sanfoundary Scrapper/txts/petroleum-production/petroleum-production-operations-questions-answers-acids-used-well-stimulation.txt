1. What is acid stimulation?
a) Treatment of a reservoir formation with fluid containing acid
b) Treatment of production tubing with a fluid containing acid
c) Treatment of a reservoir formation with fluid containing base
d) Treatment of a reservoir formation with a fluid containing the only surfactant
a
2. What is Matrix Acidizing?
a) Treatment of a reservoir formation with fluid containing a surfactant
b) When the injection pressure is more than the fracture pressure
c) Treatment of a reservoir formation with fluid containing base
d) When the injection pressure is less than the fracture pressure
d
3. What is Fracture Acidizing?
a) Treatment of a reservoir formation with fluid containing a surfactant
b) When the injection pressure is more than the fracture pressure
c) Treatment of a reservoir formation with fluid containing base
d) When the injection pressure is less than the fracture pressure
b
4. Fracture Acidizing has no application in sandstone wells.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Hydrochloric acid is normally used in the field at 40% by weight.
a) True
b) False
b
