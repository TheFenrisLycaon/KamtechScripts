1. What is the name of wet corrosion?
a) Oxidation reaction
b) Liquid metal corrosion
c)  Chemical cell
d) Electrochemical cell

2. Which of the following is not responsible for the corrosion in the oilfield equipment?

b) HCl
c) Br

3. What is the name of Dry corrosion?
a) Chemical corrosion
b) Galvanic corrosion
c) Oxidation corrosion
d) Wet corrosion
