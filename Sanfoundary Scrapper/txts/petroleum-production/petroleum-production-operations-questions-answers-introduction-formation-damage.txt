1. What is Formation damage?
a) Impairment of the permeability
b) Increased production
c) Reduced viscosity
d) Increased viscosity
a
2. Flow restrictions in the tubing or those imposed by the well or the completion geometry are also considered to be the formation damage.
a) True
b) False
b
3. In general, formation rocks are oil wet in nature.
a) True
b) False
a
4. Which of the following statement is correct regarding formation damage?
a) Formation damage can’t occur by drilling fluid
b) Productivity Index is the ratio of Flow rate by draw-down
c) Workover fluid can’t damage the formation
d) Flow efficiency is the ratio of ideal drawdown by actual drawdown
b
5. Which of the following statement is incorrect regarding formation damage?
a) Formation damage is the reduction of the natural inherent productivity of the well
b) Workover fluid, drilling fluid can occur formation damage
c) Productivity Index is the ratio of Flow rate by draw-down
d) Permeability of the formation remains unaffected by the formation damage
d
