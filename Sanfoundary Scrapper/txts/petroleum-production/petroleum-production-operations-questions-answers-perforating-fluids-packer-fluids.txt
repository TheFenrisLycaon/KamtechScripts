1. Water-based fluids are used as conventional packer fluids.

b) False

2. For the case when the high strength pipe is to be involved in completion. For any condition of temperature and fluid density which of the following fluid is recommended?
a) Oil Based Mud
b) Water Based Mud
c) Perforating Mud
d) Foam

a) Foam
b) Water Based Mud
c) Perforating Mud
d) Oil Based Mud
