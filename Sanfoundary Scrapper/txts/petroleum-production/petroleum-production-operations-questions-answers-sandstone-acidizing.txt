1. What is the primary reason to acidize the sandstone wells?
a) To increase the permeability
b) To decrease the permeability
c) To decrease the flow rate
d) To create fracture
a
2. What is the percentage of Carbonate in sandstone more than which if present, should be acidized with HCl only?
a) 5%
b) 10%
c) 20%
d) 30%
c
3. What is the basic combination of acids used in Hydrofluoric acid treatment?
a) 13% HF + 12% HCl
b) 13% HF + 12% HCl
c) 12% HF + 3% HCl
d) 3% HF + 12% HCl
d
4. To avoid CaF2 precipitation, HCl precipitation is needed in sandstone acidizing.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Diversion agents are the chemical agent used in stimulation jobs to ensure uniform injection.
a) True
b) False
a
