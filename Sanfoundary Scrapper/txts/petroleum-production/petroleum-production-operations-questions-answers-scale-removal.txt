1. Which of the following is correct regarding Potassium Carbonate?
a) Adjusts the pH of the fluid
b) Prevent a build-up of minerals in the inner wall
c) Used to lower the surface tension
d) Used to limit the growth of microbes
a
2. The uppermost valve on the Christmas tree installed above the flowline outlet is called ____
a) Tree cap
b) Pressure gauge
c) Swab valve
d) Upper master valve
c
3. What are the name of the tool that is set in the Christmas tree or wellhead and it suspends the production tubing and casing?
a) Tree nightcap
b) Flowline
c) Tubing hanger
d) Swab valve
c
4. Which of the following is correct regarding Sodium Erythorbate in hydraulic fracturing?
a) Prevents precipitation of metal oxides
b) Adjusts the pH of the fluid
c) Carrier fluid for guar gum in liquid gels
d) Product Stabilizer
a
5. Gas will migrate faster in drilling mud compared to completion brine.
a) True
b) False
b
