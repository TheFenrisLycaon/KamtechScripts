1. The bullet loses its energy and velocity when the gun clearance exceeds ______
a) 0.5 in
b) 0.5 mm
c) 0.5 cm
d) 0.5 ft
a
2. What is the size of the hole of a standard perforation?
a) 0.1 inch
b) 0.3 inch
c) 0.6 inch
d) 0.4 inch
d
3. What is the size of the depth of penetration of a standard perforation?
a) 10 inch
b) 12 inch
c) 20 inch
d) 5 ft
c
4. What is Fluid Seal Disks?
a) Helps to ignite the prima cord
b) Allows fluids to enter into the gun
c) Allows bullets to enter into the formation
d) Prevents fluids from entering into the gun
d
5. Bullets cost is much higher than Jet perforator.
a) True
b) False
b
6. What is a perforated liner?
a) A wellbore tubular with slots
b) A wellbore tubular with no slots
c) Seals the gun
d) Seals the fluid to enter into the gun
a
7. Which of the following is correct for Hydraulic perforator?
a) Hydraulic Perforation is Hydraulic fracturing
b) Perforation is obtained by fracturing
c) Cutting is obtained by edging sand-laden fluid through an orifice
d) Penetration is too high
c
8. Which of the following is correct for Mechanical Cutters in Perforation?
a) Knives and milling tools are used
b) Doesn’t make communication between the wellbore and the formation
c) Doesn’t control sand
d) Milling tools are not used
a
