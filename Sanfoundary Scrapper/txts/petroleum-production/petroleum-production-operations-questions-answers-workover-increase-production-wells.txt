1. Personnel observes that the annulus pressure on a producing well increases from 0 psi to 300 psi. The well has the packer set to isolate annuls. What does this indicator tell you?
a) The packer is leaking
b) Temperature in the annulus increases.
c) The temperature in the annulus decreases
d) The packer is leaking and there is a rise in temperature in the annulus
d
2. With gas migration in a shut-in well, which one does not increase?
a) Gas bubble pressure
b) Surface pressure
c) Bottom hole pressure
d) Shut-in drill pipe pressure
a
3. Which of the following method can be used as remedial measures for plugged perforation?
a) Breaking the perforation with ball sealer
b) Use of viscous gel
c) Reaming
d) Chemical treatment
a
