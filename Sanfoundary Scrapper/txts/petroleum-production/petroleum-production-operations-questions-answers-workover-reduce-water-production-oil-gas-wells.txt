1. Consider a formation with the pressure of 6,220 psi at a True vertical depth of 10,000 ft. If thermal expansion is there due to the heat from the well, what should be the weight of the brine is needed to balance the formation pressure?
a) Less than 12 ppg
b) More than 12 ppg
c) 12 ppg
d) 9 ppg
b
2. After killing the well by bullheading, what should be done to hold constant pressure on the formation while forward circulating is being done to displace the annulus with kill weight mud?
a) Hold tubing pressure constant while circulating
b) Hold casing pressure constant while circulating
c) Hole casing pressure constant until one tubing volume is reached and then hold tubing pressure constant
d) Start reverse circulation
a
3. Which of the following is correct regarding Potassium Carbonate used in hydraulic fracturing?
a) Adjusts the pH of fluid
b) Known as gelation chemicals
c) Used to lower the surface tension
d) Used to limit the growth of microbes
a

b) False

b) False
