1. The prestressed concrete structures pressure vessels can be triaxially prestressed resulting in ____________
a) Reflection
b) High load factors
c) Tensile factors
d) Ductile factors
b
2. One of the nuclear power station located in Tamilnadu shielded by a double containment system is?
a) Lankesh nuclear power station
b) Gauri nuclear power station
c) Kalpakkam nuclear power station
d) Radant nuclear power station
c
3. The maximum credible accident (MCA) is a ____________
a) Nuclear containment structures
b) Explosive container
c) Hydrogen container
d) Mica container
a
4. The atomic power reactor at Rajasthan was built in which year?
a) 1900
b) 1935
c) 1965
d) 1340
c
5. One of the prestressed power reactor vessels in the following.
a) Madras
b) Assam
c) Tripura
d) Jharkhand
a
6. The prestressing system of the dome in madras power atomic reactor vessel comprises of how many brands?
a) 4
b) 3
c) 2
d) 1
b
7. The Narora atomic power plant consists of a ____________
a) Single containment vessel
b) Triple containment
c) Prestressed containment vessel
d) Double containment vessel
d
8. The prestressed concrete silos are generally preferred for storage of ____________
a) Blocks
b) Anchors
c) Fertilizers
d) Concrete
c
9. The thickness of concrete in the shell is in the range of ____________
a) 60-70mm
b) 50-75mm
c) 90-120mm
d) 40-50mm

10. The economical concrete silos shape is ____________
a) Paraboloid shell shape
b) Ellipse shell shape
c) Circle shell shape
d) Dome shell shape
