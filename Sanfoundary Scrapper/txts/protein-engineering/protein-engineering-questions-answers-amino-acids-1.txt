1. Which of the following amino acid is acidic?
a) Asparagine
b) Leucine
c) Valine
d) Aspartic acid
d
2. What is the three-letter code of Glutamine?
a) Glu
b) Gly
c) Arg
d) Gln
d

a) Tyr
b) Asn
c) Trp
d) Thr
d

a) Leucine
b) Alanine
c) Tryptophan
d) Glycine
d
