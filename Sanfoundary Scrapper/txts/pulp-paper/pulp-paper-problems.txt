1.	What is the full form of M.E.E?
a)	Multiple-effect evaporators
b)	Molecular-energy evaporators
c)	Multiple –entropy evaporators
d)	Molecular-engineering evaporators
a
2.	If 100lb of black liquor containing 15lb of solids and 85lb of water is evaporated to 30lb; with a stream economy of 5 it would take 14lb of steam to remove the 70lb of water. The steam economy is about 0.8 per effect. An overall steam economy of the system is ?
a)	9-10
b)	4-5
c)	20-25
d)	16-17
b
3.	Stream economy equals water mass evaporated by the mass of stream used.
a)	True
b)	False
a
4.	What is the full form of L.T.V?
a)	Long tube vertical
b)	Less tube vertical
c)	Leakage tube vent
d)	Lombarda tube vent
a
5.	Falling film evaporators are used in conventional evaporators except for the mechanism for evaporation in each stage is of different unit.
a)	True
b)	False
a
6.	About 65% of the methanol and BOD is concentrated in 6% of the entire condensate stream in the upper portion of the plates just to release __________
a)	Four condensate segregation
b)	Fine copper segregation
c)	Foul condensate segregation
d)	Free condensate segregation
c
7.	Tall oil is a by-product mixture of the saponified fatty acids, resin acids and unsaponifiables which are derived from the wood extractives of ___________
a)	Hardwood
b)	Softwood
c)	Turpentine
d)	Lignin
b
8.	___________ is a mixture of volatile extractives collected during digester heating.
a)	Kraft lignin
b)	Tall oil
c)	Wax
d)	Turpentine
d
9.	___________ is recovered by acidification and used dispersants, phenol-formaldehyde adhesive extenders, and binders in printing inks.
a)	White liquor
b)	Brown liquor
c)	Black liquor
d)	Green liquor
c
10.	Indirect concentrators are forced circulation used to concentrate black liquor for the range where burkeite precipitates as scale. Where are they used?
a)	Low-odour recovery boilers
b)	High-odour recovery boilers
c)	Constant-odour recovery boiler
d)	Burst-odour recovery boiler
a
