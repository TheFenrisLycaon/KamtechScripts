1. In Boot-strap air cooling system how many heat exchangers are there?
a) 1
b) 2
c) 3
d) 0
b
2. Which of the following is not a process in the T-s diagram of the Boot-strap air cooling system?
a) Isentropic ramming
b) Isentropic compression
c) Cooling of ram air
d) Isothermal expansion
d
3. What is the difference between Boot-strap air cooling system and Boot-strap evaporative cooling system?
a) Boot strap evaporative system has an evaporator
b) Boot strap evaporative system has two evaporators
c) Boot-strap evaporator eliminates the need for evaporator
d) Boot-strap evaporator system has three evaporators
a
4. Mass of air per tonne of refrigeration will be _____ in Boot-strap air cooling system than Boot-strap evaporative system.
a) less
b) more
c) equal to
d) can’t say
b
5. The air cooling system that is used mostly in transport type aircraft is?
a) Boot-strap air cooling system
b) Simple air cooling system
c) Simple evaporative cooling system
d) Regenerative air cooling system
a
6. A Boot-strap air cooling system is used for a transport aircraft to take 10TR of refrigeration load (Q). The power required for the refrigerating system P is 800 KW. What is its COP?
a) 0.06426
b) 0.04375
c) 0.05435
d) 0.04367
b
COP = \(\frac{210×10}{800×60}\) 
= 0.04375.
