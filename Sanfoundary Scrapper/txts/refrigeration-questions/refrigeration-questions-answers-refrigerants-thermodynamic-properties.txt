1. Among the following refrigerants, which is having the lowest freezing point?
a) R – 21
b) R – 11
c) R – 12
d) R – 22
d
2. What is the boiling point of Ammonia at atmospheric pressure?
a) -10.5°C
b) -30°C
c) -33.3°C
d) -77.7°C
c
3. R – 717 is the refrigerant number of which of the following refrigerant?
a) Ammonia
b) Water
c) Carbon dioxide
d) Sulphur dioxide
a
Explanation R – 717 is the refrigerant number of Ammonia. Whereas, refrigerant number of Water, Carbon dioxide, and Sulphur dioxide is R – 118, R – 744 and R – 764 respectively.
4. What is the boiling point of Carbon dioxide at atmospheric pressure?
a) -20.5°C
b) -73.6°C
c) -50°C
d) -68°C
b
5. What is the freezing point of R – 12 at atmospheric pressure?
a) -86.6°C
b) -73.6°C
c) -157.5°C
d) -160°C
c
6. What is the refrigerant number of Water?
a) R – 717
b) R – 744
c) R – 118
d) R – 100
c
7. Among the following refrigerants, which is having the lowest boiling point?
a) Ammonia
b) R – 12
c) Carbon dioxide
d) Sulphur dioxide
c
8. Among the following refrigerants, which is having the lowest C.O.P for refrigeration system working under the temperature limits of -15°C and 30°C as evaporator and condenser temperature respectively?
a) Ammonia
b) R – 12
c) R – 30
d) Carbon dioxide
c
9. R – 11 is having the highest C.O.P for refrigeration system working under the temperature limits of -15°C and 30°C as evaporator and condenser temperature respectively.
a) False
b) True
b
10. A refrigerant should have a low latent heat of vaporization at the evaporator temperature.
a) True
b) False
b
