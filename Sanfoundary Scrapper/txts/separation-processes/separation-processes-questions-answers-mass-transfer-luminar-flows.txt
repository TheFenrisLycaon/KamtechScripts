1. The equation of motion for the liquid film in fully developed laminar flow in y-direction is:
a) µd2uy/dx2 + ρg =0
b) µd2uy/dy2 + ρg =0
c) µd2uy/dz2 + ρg =0
d) µd2uy/dx2 = 0
c
Hence the equation of motion is µd2uy/dz2 + ρg =0.
