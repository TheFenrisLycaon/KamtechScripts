1. The economical spacing of roof trusses works out to be _________ span.
a) 1/2 to 1/5 span
b) 1/3 to 1/5 span
c) 1/2 to 1/3 span
d) 1/4 to 1/6 span
b
2. The top chord members of roof truss is called ___________
a) Common rafters
b) Principal rafters
c) Main tie
d) Pitch
b
3. The bottom chord member of truss is known as ___________
a) Main tie
b) Principal rafters
c) Common rafters
d) Purlins
a
4. _______ is a roof beam supported by roof truss.
a) Ridge line
b) Eve
c) Principal rafter

a) Slope
b) Splay
c) Pitch
d) Tie

6. The angle of repose is zero for ______________
a) Water
b) Masonry
c) Soil
d) Cement

a) 10
b) 15
c) 5
d) 0
d

a) Magnesium sulphate
b) Alum
c) Chlorine
d) Salt

a) Sedimentation
b) Flocculation
c) Coagulation

a) Sedimentation
b) Filtration

d) Aeration

a) Mechanical straining
b) Cascades
c) Biological metabolism
d) Electrolytic changes

b) As free chlorine gas

b) E
c) K
