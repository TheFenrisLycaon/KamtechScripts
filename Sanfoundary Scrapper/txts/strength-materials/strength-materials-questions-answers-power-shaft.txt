1. Calculate the power transmitted in the shaft at 150 rpm. Take torque as 9000Nm.
a) 140 kW
b) 150 kW
c) 160 kW
d) 175 kW
a
P = 2 π × 150 × 9000 / 60
P = 140 kW.
