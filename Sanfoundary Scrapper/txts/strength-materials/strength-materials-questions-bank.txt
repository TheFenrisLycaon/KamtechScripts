1. The longitudinal stress is _____ stress across the section.
a) Shear
b) Bending
c) Tensile
d) Compressive
c
2. The longitudinal stress in the shell is _________
a) pd/3t
b) pd/4t
c) pd/2t
d) pd/6t
b
f = p × π/4 d2 / π d t
f = pd/4t.
