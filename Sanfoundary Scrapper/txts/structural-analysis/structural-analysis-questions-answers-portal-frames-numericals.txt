1. Vertical reaction at support B for the given frame is ______
a) 10kN (Upward)
b) 10kN (Downward)
c) 26kN (Upward)
d) 26kN (Downward)
c
∑Mb=0
Vb*3-36*1.5-24*1=0
Vb=26kN (Upward)
2. Vertical reaction at support A for the given frame is ______
a) 10kN (Upward)
b) 10kN (Downward)
c) 26kN (Upward)
d) 26kN (Downward)
a
∑V=0
Va+Vb=36kN
Vb=36-26=10kN (Downward)
