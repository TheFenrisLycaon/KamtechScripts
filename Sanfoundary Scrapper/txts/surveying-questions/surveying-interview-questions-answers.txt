1. What is the length of the revenue chain?
a) 100ft
b) 33ft
c) 66ft
d) 50ft
b
2. How many numbers of links in case of revenue chain?
a) 10
b) 12
c) 16
d) 33
c
3. What is the length of each link in case of revenue chain?
a) 1 ft
b) 2 ft
c) 2.0625 ft
d) 1.0625ft
c
4. Cloth tapes of closely woven linen, 12 to 15 mm wide varnished to resist _______
a) Shrinkage
b) Heat due to sunlight
c) Moisture
d) Rusting
c
5. Which of the following tape is not made?
a) Linen tape
b) Invar tape
c) Steel tape
d) Iron tape
d
6. What are used after every chain length measured on the ground?
a) Pegs
b) Ranging rods
c) Arrows
d) Offset rods
c
7. Length of arrow may vary from _______ to ______
a) 25 cm to 30 cm
b) 20 cm to 50 cm
c) 35 cm to 50 cm
d) 25 cm to 50 cm
d
8. Diameter of ring of arrow is ________
a) 50 mm
b) 25 mm
c) 30 mm
d) 25 mm
a
9. What are used to mark the positions of the stations or terminal points of a survey line?
a) Arrows
b) Pegs
c) Ranging rods
d) Offset rods
b
10. Pegs are driven in the ground with the help of a wooden hammer and kept about _________ cm projecting above the surface.
a) 3 cm
b) 2 cm
c) 1 cm
d) 4 cm
d
11. What is the length of Ranging rod commonly used?
a) 2 m
b) 3 m
c) 4 m
d) 5 m
a
12. What is the length of each band in ranging rod?
a) 10 cm
b) 20 cm
c) 30 cm
d) 40 cm
b
13. Rods are almost invisible at a distance of about ______
a) 100 m
b) 50 m
c) 150 m
d) 200 m
d
14. Which of the following is used for Ranging out a line when crossing a depression from which the forward rod is invisible, or when it is hidden by the obstacles, such as hedges?
a) Peg
b) Ranging rods
c) Butt rod
d) Latha
d
15. Pieces of sharpened thin sticks cut from the nearest edge, and are used for the same purpose as the laths are called _______
a) Pegs
b) Whites
c) Butt rod
d) Plumb bob
b
