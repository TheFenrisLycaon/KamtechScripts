1. The horizontal angle between the true meridian and the magnetic meridian shown by needle at the time of observation is called ______
a) True bearing
b) Magnetic bearing
c) Arbitrary bearing
d) Magnetic declination
d
2. If the magnetic meridian is to the right side of the true meridian, declination is said to the _________
a) eastern
b) western
c) southern
d) northern
a
3. If the magnetic meridian is to the left side of the true meridian, declination is said to the _________
a) eastern
b) western
c) southern
d) northern
b
4. If the magnetic meridian is to the right side of the true meridian, declination is said to the positive.
a) True
b) False
a
5. If the magnetic meridian is to the left side of the true meridian, declination is said to the negative.
a) True
b) False
a
6. What is the systematic departure of the declination from its mean value during a period of 24 hours?
a) Diurnal variation
b) Annual variation
c) Secular variation
d) Irregular variation
a
7. The variation which has a yearly period is known as _________ variation.
a) Diurnal variation
b) Annual variation
c) Secular variation
d) Irregular variation
b
8. Which of the following variation to appear to be of the periodic character and follows a sine curve pattern?
a) Diurnal variation
b) Annual variation
c) Secular variation
d) Irregular variation
c
9. Which of the following variations are due to magnetic storms?
a) Diurnal variation
b) Annual variation
c) Secular variation
d) Irregular variation
d
10. Find the magnetic declination at a place if the magnetic bearing of the sun at noon is 188°?
a) 8° W
b) 4° W
c) 8° E
d) 4° E
a
