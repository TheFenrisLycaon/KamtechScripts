1. Strength of signal doesn’t depend upon which of the following factors?
a) Energy flux
b) Dwell time
c) Altitude
d) Reflection
d
2.  Which of the following indicates the correct set of bands operated in LISS- III satellite?
a) Visible ray, near IR
b) Near IR, radio wave
c) Radio wave, near IR
d) Far IR, near IR
a
3. Energy flux may affect which of the following?
a) Lens
b) Strength of the signal
c) Aperture
d) Declination
b
4. Which of the following can merge the imagery of LISS-III and PAN?
a) IRS 1B
b) IRS 1A
c) IRS 1C
d) IRS multi sensor
d
5. Which among the following indicates the correct expansion of WiFS?
a) Wide Field Sensor
b) Wireless Fidelity Sensor
c) Wide Fidelity Sensor
d) Wireless Field Sensor
a
6. IRS P3 satellite uses which of the following sensors?
a) PAN
b) LISS-III
c) MOS
d) LISS-II
c
7. PAN sensor uses two band operators.
a) True
b) False
b
8. While mapping land use and land cover, which scale is recommended?
a) 1: 25000
b) 1: 50000
c) 1: 250000
d) 1: 25
a
9. The sensor used in the digital elevation model can identify contour heights greater than _____
a) 5 m
b) 30 m
c) 20 m
d) 10 m
d
10. IRS 1A and 1B satellites can carry which of the following sensors?
a) LISS-IV
b) LISS-III
c) LISS-I
d) LISS-V
c
