1. Which of the following indicates the use of Zenith telescope?
a) Zenith placement
b) Zero error
c) Low precision
d) High precision
d
2. Zenith telescope also known as __________
a) Zenith sector
b) Sextant
c) Zenith horizon
d) Azimuthal telescope
a
3. Which of the following describes the usage of zenith telescope?
a) Determination of equator
b) Determination of earth poles
c) Determination of tropic of Capricorn
d) Determination of tropic of cancer
b
4. Large zenith telescope is having a diameter of range ___________
a) 0.5-2m
b) 1-2m
c) 2-2.5m
d) 3-4m
d
5. Zenith telescope has been replaced by ___________
a) Celestial telescope
b) Astronomical telescope
c) Radio astronomical quasar
d) Abnormal telescope
c
6. Zenith telescope can track the points other than its line of sight.
a) True
b) False
b
7. Large zenith telescope is used at ___________
a) Australia
b) India
c) South America
d) North America
d
8. Which of the following indicates the objective of the zenith telescope?
a) Spectral energy consumption
b) Spectral energy dissolution
c) Spectral energy distribution
d) Spectral energy reflection
c
9. Zenith telescope is more expensive.
a) False
b) True
a
10. Who among the following theorized zenith telescope for the first time?
a) Sir Isaac Newton
b) Albert Einstein
c) Ramanujan
d) Faraday
a
