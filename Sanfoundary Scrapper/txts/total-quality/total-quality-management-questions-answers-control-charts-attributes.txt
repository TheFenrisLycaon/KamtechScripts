1. The control chart that determines the fraction of rejected parts as non-conforming is ______
a) R-chart
b) S-chart
c) P-chart
d) C-chart
c
2. The total number of parts in ten samples of equal size is 1200. What is the average sample size?
a) 120
b) 12
c) 1.2
d) 1200
a
3. The total number of defect count is 200 and the total number of parts in ten samples of equal size is 1500. What is the fraction defective?
a) 0.131
b) 0.134
c) 0.137
d) 0.140
b
4. The p-chart is used when subgroup size is variable.
a) True
b) False
a

b) S-chart
c) P-chart
d) C-chart

6. What is the basis for C-chart?
a) Normal distribution
b) Poisson distribution
c) Weibull distribution
d) Exponential distribution
b

a) True
b) False
a
8. The control chart that measures the number of non-conformities is ____ and the one that measures the number of non-conformities per unit is ______
a) R-chart, S-chart
b) C-chart, U-chart
c) P-chart, R-chart
