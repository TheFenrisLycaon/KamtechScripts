1. Which of the following means ‘visual cards’?
a) Poka-yoke
b) 5S
c) Muda
d) Kanban
d
2. C-Kanban is also known as _______
a) P- Kanban
b) Production Kanban
c) Withdrawal Kanban
d) Poka-Yoke
c
3. Withdrawal Kanban is also known as Move Kanban.
a) True
b) False
a
4. P-Kanban is also known as Production Kanban.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Calculate number of Kanbans if the hourly demand is 200 units, lead time is 12 hours, container capacity is 144 units, and variation in lead time is 15%.
a) 23
b) 22
c) 21
d) 20
d
6. _______ signals the need to produce more parts and ______ signals the need to deliver more parts to the next work center.
a) P-Kanban, C-Kanban
b) P-Kanban, P-Kanban
c) C-Kanban, C-Kanban
d) C-Kanban, P-Kanban
a
7. Which of the following does not belong to the five principles of lean?
a) Identifying Value
b) Mapping the value stream
c) Creating flow
d) Establishing push
d
8. Which of the following is not an example of visual factory tools?
a) Visual indicators
b) Visual displays
c) Visual controls
d) Benchmarking
d
9. Which of the following is not a benefit of a visual factory?
a) Safer workplace
b) Improved productivity
c) Lower profits
d) Improved output
c
