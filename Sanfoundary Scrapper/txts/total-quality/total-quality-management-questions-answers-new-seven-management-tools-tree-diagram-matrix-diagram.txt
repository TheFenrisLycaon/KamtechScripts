1. The tree diagram is break down of a topic into its component elements, showing the logical and sequential links between these elements in a systematic manner.
a) True
b) False
a
2. Quality table is also known as _______
a) Relationship diagram
b) Tree diagram
c) Matrix diagram
d) Arrow diagram
c
3. Matrix diagram is the starting point in building the house of quality.
a) True
b) False
a
4. Which of the following is not a type of matrix diagram?
a) L shaped
b) T shaped
c) Roof shaped
d) Ground shaped
d
5. _______ matrix diagram is used to relate two groups of items to each other (or one group to itself).
a) T-shaped
b) L-shaped
c) Y-shaped
d) Roof-shaped
b
6. ______ matrix diagram is used to relate three groups of items; each group is related to the other two in a circular fashion.
a) T-shaped
b) L-shaped
c) Y-shaped
d) Roof-shaped
c
7. _____ matrix diagram is used to relate four group of items; each group is related to two others in a circular fashion.
a) T-shaped
b) X-shaped
c) Y-shaped
d) Roof-shaped
b
8. _____ matrix diagram is used to relate three groups of items all together simultaneously in 3-D.
a) T-shaped
b) X-shaped
c) Y-shaped
d) C-shaped
