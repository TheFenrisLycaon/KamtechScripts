1. Two suppliers A and B deliver the same raw materials for production company Z. The price offered by supplier A is less compared to the price offered by supplier B. Which supplier should the company choose?
a) Supplier A
b) Supplier B
c) Supplier A & Supplier B
d) Depends on other factors like quality
d
2. Two suppliers A and B deliver the same raw materials for production company Z. The price offered by supplier A is less compared to the price offered by supplier B. The quality of both the raw materials is the same. Which supplier should the company choose considering other factors remaining the same for both of them?
a) Supplier A
b) Supplier B
c) Supplier A & Supplier B
d) None of them
a
3. Which of the following is not the primary factor on which the supplier is selected by an organization?
a) Cost
b) Quality
c) Delivery reliability
d) Language barrier
d
4. Additionally, the supplier selection criteria include factors such as management compatibility, goal congruence, and strategic decision of the supplier firm.
a) True
b) False
a
5. Survey stage, enquiry stage, negotiation and selection stage, and experience stage are the four stages in supplier selection and evaluation.
a) True
b) False
a
6. ________ suggested the ten conditions for selection and evaluation of suppliers.
a) Ishikawa
b) Taguchi
c) Juran
d) Deming
a
a) Survey stage
b) Enquiry stage
c) Negotiation and selection stage
d) Experience stage
a
8. In which of the following stages of supplier selection and evaluation, vendor’s present customers are enquired about the performance and promptness in delivery of the vendor?
a) Survey stage
b) Enquiry stage
c) Negotiation and selection stage
d) Experience stage
b
