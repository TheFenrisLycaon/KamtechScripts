1. Which of the following is not an application of speed study?
a) Determining appropriate speed limits
b) Analyzing the cause of accidents
c) Deciding speed trends
d) For deciding PCU
d
2. Why are speed trends at local, state, and national level established?
a) To standardize a speed limit
b) To compare the results
c) To assess the effectiveness of the national strategies on speed limits
d) For design purposes
c
3. Cost-benefit analysis of a highway project is an application of speed study.
a) True
b) False
a
4. With the help of speed studies, traffic operations are controlled and regulated to ______
a) establish safe speed limits
b) analyze traffic capacity
c) analyze the cause of accidents
d) decide speed trends
a
