1. Fixed delay is caused by ________
a) control devices
b) impedance of traffic
c) when the vehicle is at rest
d) overtaking
a
2. Why control devices like traffic signals should not be overused?
a) To reduce fixed delay
b) To reduce operational delay
c) To reduce stopped-time delay
d) To reduce excessive delay
a
3. Level crossing causes fixed delay.
a) True
b) False
a
4. Fixed delay does not depend on which of the following factor?
a) Traffic volume
b) Traffic signals
c) Level crossing
d) Markers
a
