1. The hourly volume that is exceeded only 29 times a year is known as _______
a) 30th highest hourly volume
b) Peak hourly volume
c) AADT
d) ADT
a
2. What is directional volume?
a) Peak hour volume
b) 30th highest hourly volume
c) AADT
d) ADT
a
3. When are reversible lanes used?
a) When the directional imbalance is significant
b) When the directional imbalance is insignificant
c) When the traffic signal doesn’t work in one lane
d) When there is a blockade
a
4. Peak hourly volume is smaller than Design hourly volume.
a) True
b) False
a
5. What happens when recommendations of traffic impact studies that use the Design hourly volume (DHV) is implemented in the Peak hour warrant?
a) Congestion
b) Installation of unwarranted traffic signals
c) Economical design of highway
d) Satisfactory design of highway
b
6. How is the design year determined?
a) Roadway network planning schedule
b) City planning schedule
c) National planning schedule
d) International planning schedule
a
7. The maximum hourly traffic volume that a highway should be designed to serve without the level of service falling below a predetermined level is known as ______
a) AADT
b) 30th highest hourly volume
c) Design service volume
d) Peak hour volume
c
8. It is agreeable to choose an hourly volume for design, which is about 50 percent of the volumes expected to occur during the few highest hours of the design year, whether or not that volume is equal to the 30th highest hourly volume.
a) True
b) False
a
