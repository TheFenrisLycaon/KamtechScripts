1. What is gap acceptance?
a) Maximum gap required to finish lane changing safely
b) Minimum major-stream headway during which a minor-street vehicle can make a manoeuvre
c) Time interval between the arrival of two cars
d) Minimum gap required to finish lane changing safely
d
2. Which of the following equation can be used to determine the expected number of adequate gaps that will take place at an unsignalized intersection or at the merging area of an expressway?
a) P(0) = P(h>0, h=0) = e—λt
b) P(0) = P(h=0) = e—λt
c) P(0) x P(h>0) = e—λt
d) P(0) = P(h>0) = e—λt
a
3. Which of the following is determined by gap acceptance?
a) To determine the delay of vehicles in minor streams aiming to merge onto major streams
b) To determine the maximum gap required to finish lane changing safely
c) To determine the minimum gap required to finish lane changing safely
d) To determine minimum major-stream headway during which a minor-street vehicle can make a manoeuvre
a
4. Which of the following is not a parameter of traffic stream?
a) Density of traffic
b) Flow of traffic
c) Speed
d) PCU
d
5. Which of the following is the parameter of traffic stream that has microscopic characteristics?
a) Flow
b) Density
c) Speed
d) Space headway
d
