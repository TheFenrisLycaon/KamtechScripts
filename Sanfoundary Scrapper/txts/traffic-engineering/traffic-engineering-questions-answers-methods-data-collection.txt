1. How many methods are used for the origin-destination study?
a) 6
b) 1
c) 5
d) 4
a
2. Which among the following is an origin-destination study?
a) Licence plate mail-out method
b) Traffic mass study
c) Floating car method
d) Average speed method
a
3. In which of the following origin-destination study licence plate numbers of vehicles are recorded?
a) Licence plate mail-out method
b) Roadside interview
c) Phone survey
d) Mail back postcard survey
a
4. How many methods can be used for obtaining licence plate number of the vehicles in Licence plate mail-out method of origin-destination study?
a) 2
b) 3
c) 4
d) 5
a
5. Which among the four method is preferred for high volume highways?
a) Photo/video
b) Manual recording
c) Interview
d) Questionnaire
a
6. Which of the following method has no disruption to normal traffic flow?
a) Licence plate mail-out method
b) Roadside interview
c) Questionnaire
d) Toll booths
a
7. Which of the following origin-destination study requires directing vehicles to a designated interview area and asking a series of short questions?
a) Licence plate mail-out method
b) Roadside interview
c) Phone survey
d) Mail back postcard survey
b
8. Which of the following is not an advantage of Roadside interview method?
a) High response rate
b) Good sampling control
c) Broad geographic coverage
d) No interruption to traffic
d
9. Roadside interview method is widely used for _________
a) Truck and auto trip collection
b) Village roads
c) National highways
d) Mountainous roads
a
10. Which of the following is the primary disadvantage of Roadside interview method?
a) Traffic disruption
b) Requires more manpower
c) Skilled surveyors
d) Huge set of data
a
11. When is mail-back postcard surveys usually used?
a) When traffic volume is high
b) When traffic volume is low
c) When traffic is homogeneous
d) When traffic is heterogeneous
b
12. How is mail-back postcard surveys conducted?
a) Postcard with a brief questionnaire
b) Via email
c) Via post
d) Postcard with an elaborate questionnaire
a
13. Which of the following is an advantage of the mail-back postcard survey?
a) Less personnel required
b) Skilled personnel required
c) More personnel required
d) High response rate
a
14. Phone surveys are usually used for ___________
a) household travel survey
b) pedestrian survey
c) traffic volume
d) traffic flow
a
15. Which of the following method used cell phone tracking technology?
a) Phone surveys
b) Mail-back postcard survey
c) Road interview method
d) GPS
d
