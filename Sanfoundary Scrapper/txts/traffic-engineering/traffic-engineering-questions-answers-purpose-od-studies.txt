1. Which of the following is not an objective of OD Studies?
a) To determine the amount of by passable traffic that enters a town and thus establishes the need for diversion
b) To develop trip generation and trip distribution models
c) To determine the potential of the present highway system and plan for new facilities
d) To assess the pedestrian pattern
d
2. What do you mean by trips?
a) One-way movement of a person from origin to destination
b) Two-way movement of a person from origin to destination
c) Continuous movement
d) A movement that is more than 50 kms
a
3. Trips are classified into how many categories?
a) 2
b) 3
c) 4
d) 5
b
4. Which one of the following is not a common application OD survey?
a) Weaving-area studies
b) Freeway studies
c) Major activity centre studies
d) Traffic volume studies
d
5. OD surveys are conducted to study the pattern of persons and goods in a specific area of study during a certain period of time.
a) True
b) False
a
6. Why are screen lines required?
a) To check the accuracy
b) To divide different types of vehicles
c) To divide different types of goods and passengers
d) To divide areas into urban and rural
a
