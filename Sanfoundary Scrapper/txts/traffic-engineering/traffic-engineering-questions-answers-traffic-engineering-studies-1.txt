1. Which of the following is not a category of Traffic Studies?
a) Inventories
b) Administrative studies
c) Dynamic studies
d) Economic studies
d
2. Which of the following traffic study requires the collection of data under operational conditions?
a) Dynamic studies
b) Administrative studies
c) Inventories
d) Economic studies
a
3. Spot speed studies are conducted to ______
a) estimate distribution of speeds of vehicles in traffic at a particular location
b) estimate distribution of traffic density at a particular location
c) survey various locations based on speed
d) survey various locations based on traffic density
a
4. Aerial photography and surveying are required in which type of traffic study?
a) Inventories
b) Administrative studies
c) Dynamic studies
d) Detailed study
b
5. Goods movement and transit study is a type of traffic study.
a) True
b) False
a
6. Pneumatic road tube device is a type of _______
a) Manual data collection method
b) Semi-automated data collection method
c) Fully-automated data collection method
d) None of the above
b
7. The Fully automated study makes use of portable devices and sensors.
a) True
b) False
b
8. Which of the following study is not conducted manually?
a) Traffic counts
b) Intersection delay
c) Operation of the traffic signal
d) Travel time data
c
9. Which of the following is the prime disadvantage of fixed signal?
a) Data is available for a short period of time
b) It is not portable
c) Requires skills to use
d) Costly
a
10. What is the error reported when using a stopwatch for manual study?
a) Time error
b) Parallax
c) Reading error
d) Cumulative error
b
11. What principle does radar meters use?
a) Doppler principle
b) Van-Hoff principle
c) Hooke’s law
d) Tyndall effect
a
12. Radar meters must have a license from ________
a) Indian Traffic Commission
b) Federal Communication Commission
c) Federal Traffic Commission
d) Indian Communication Commission
b
13. Pneumatic tubes are used for ______
a) Traffic counting
b) Surveying
c) Parking counts
d) Delay time
a
14. What is the disadvantage of Pneumatic tubes?
a) They are costly
b) They require skill
c) They count the number of axles
d) They are not portable
.
