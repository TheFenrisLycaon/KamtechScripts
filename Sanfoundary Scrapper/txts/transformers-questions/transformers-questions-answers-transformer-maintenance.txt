c) 2
d) Many

2. Ingress of air into the oil tank can be avoided by using ___________
a) relays
b) plastic coatings
c) metal coatings
d) fuses
a
3. When tripping of the transformer from the main circuit is required?
a) Local overheating
b) Short-circuited core laminations
c) Core-bolt insulation failure
d) Puncture of bushings
d
Explanations: For all other options removing transformer from the circuit is not necessary as alarms and consequent actions help to remove that fault. While in following four cases transformer must be removed from the main circuitry: (a) short-circuit between phases, (b) winding earth fault, (c) winding short-circuit, (d) puncture of bushings.
