1. Single-stranded DNA was used in __________
a)	Dideoxy method
b)	Replication
c)	Translational

a)	Strand separation
b)	Replication

3. RF acts just like a ___________
a)	Phage
b)	Host
c)	Plasmid
d)	Hybrid

b)	Transfect

b)	False
