1. Why is a eukaryotic cloning vector required?
a)	Cloning ease
b)	Variety
c)	Availability
d)	Large scale production
d
2. Saccharomyces cerevisiae is an example of _________
a)	Fungi
b)	Yeast
c)	Bacteria
d)	Insect
b
3. Plasmids are present in eukaryotes.
a)	True
b)	False
a
4. The 2 micrometer circle found in yeast is a __________
a)	Phage
b)	Plasmid
c)	Virus
d)	Gene product
b
5. What is the size of 2 micrometer circle?
a)	1 kb
b)	6 kb
c)	11 kb
d)	22 kb
b

b)	Replication

a)	Antibiotic
b)	Lac

b)	Transformed cell
c)	Host cell

a)	Lactose
b)	Leucine
c)	Dehydrogenase
d)	Hydrogenase

a)	Luria Broth
b)	Agar
