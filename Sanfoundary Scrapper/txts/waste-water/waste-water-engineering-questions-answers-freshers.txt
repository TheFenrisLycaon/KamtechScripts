1.	Turbidity in water is caused by which of these following?
a)	Total dissolved solids
b)	Suspended solids
c)	Ions
d)	Heavy metals
b

a)	TSS = (2.3) x Turbidity
b)	TSS = (2.0) x Turbidity
c)	TSS = (2.5) x Turbidity
d)	TSS = (1.3) x Turbidity
a

a)	Hardness
b)	Suspended solids

b)	Total Dissolved Solids

b)	Arsenic
