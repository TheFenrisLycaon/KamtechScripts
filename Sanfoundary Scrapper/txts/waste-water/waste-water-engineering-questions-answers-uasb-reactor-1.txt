1.	UASB is an anaerobic reactor.
a)	True
b)	False
a
2.	UASB is only used for lower levels of treatment.
a)	True
b)	False
b
3.	How does the wastewater enter the reactor?
a)	Bottom
b)	Top
c)	Side
d)	Top and side
a
4.	A sludge blanket in the reactor is ______
a)	At the top
b)	At the bottom
c)	Absent
d)	In between
d
5.	_____ breaks down the organic matter.
a)	Bacteria
b)	Fungi
c)	Virus
d)	Algae
a

a)	Rubber cock
b)	Out pipe
c)	Baffle
d)	Stopper

a)	0.3-0.6 mm
b)	0.5-0.8 mm
c)	1-3 mm
d)	3-6 mm
c

a)	Methane
